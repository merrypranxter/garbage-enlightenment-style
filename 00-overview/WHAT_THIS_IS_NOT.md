# WHAT THIS IS NOT
This file defines the major things Garbage Enlightenment should not be mistaken for.
This matters because the style has enough overlap with neighboring categories that it can be
mislabeled or misgenerated very easily.
## 1. NOT GENERIC PSYCHEDELIC ART
Garbage Enlightenment is not simply:
- rainbow weirdness
- altered-state decoration
- generic hallucination vibes
- abstract trippy patterning
Why:
It is much more specific in its use of:
- body glyphs
- mock esoterica
- Sweet Corruption
- rude linework
- mutation logic

- figure-pattern permeability
## 2. NOT VISIONARY ART
It is not:
- spiritually reverent
- polished sacred art
- majestic cosmic revelation painting
- beauty-first transcendence imagery
Why:
The style frequently undercuts revelation with cartoon absurdity, cheapness, bodily
contamination, or symbolic stupidity.
## 3. NOT CLEAN KAWAII
It is not:
- pristine cuteness
- polished mascots
- harmless sticker sweetness
- pure softness
Why:
Cute motifs are allowed, but they are usually exposed to weirdness, contamination, mutation, or
symbolic tension.
## 4. NOT PURE BODY HORROR
It is not:
- realistic gore
- flesh realism
- clinical disgust
- sadistic horror art
- oppressive monster illustration
Why:
The bodily element here is symbolic, cartoonized, graphic, playful, and transformable, even
when gross.
## 5. NOT CORPORATE PLAYFUL ILLUSTRATION
It is not:
- startup blob art
- clean editorial vector design

- office-friendly weirdness
- sanitized modern “quirky” illustration
Why:
The style is too body-haunted, too rude, too symbolically unstable, and too low-fi for that.
## 6. NOT POP SURREALISM LUXURY MODE
It is not:
- airbrushed dream painting
- rendered lowbrow gallery finish
- pretty oddness
- expensive-looking weirdness
Why:
Garbage Enlightenment wants bluntness and mutation pressure more than polish.
## 7. NOT ORNATE PSYCHEDELIC POSTER FILIGREE
It is not:
- lettering-led 60s poster design
- Art Nouveau psychedelic ornament systems
- decorative psychedelic flourishes as the main event
Why:
This repo is more about symbolic cartoon mutation than ornamental flourishes.
## 8. NOT SLICK OCCULT BRANDING
It is not:
- perfect eye-in-triangle merch
- black-and-gold luxury esoteric design
- stylish grim icon systems
- occult design with too much authority
Why:
The style does use these symbols, but usually in parody, contamination, or bodily weirdness
mode.
## 9. NOT RANDOM ABSURDIST DOODLE ART
It is not:
- meaningless weirdness
- chaos for chaos’s sake

- disconnected surreal fragments with no family logic
Why:
The repo has a specific symbolic ecosystem and repeated internal laws.
## 10. NOT A LITERAL SIMULATION OF LSD VISUALS
This is important.
Garbage Enlightenment may resemble **cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic** in:
- rapid symbolic reassignment
- pattern-to-figure emergence
- over-significance
- morph-heavy identity instability
- emotionally charged visual substitution
But it is **not** trying to be:
- clinically accurate psychedelic depiction
- neuroscience illustration
- first-person hallucination documentary realism
- faithfulness-to-actual-trip optics above all else
It is using some of that logic as raw material and compressing it into:
- rude cartoon forms
- symbolic body language
- parody esoterica
- cheap synthetic graphic systems
## FINAL RULE
Garbage Enlightenment is not best understood by asking:
“What broad art genre does this resemble?”
It is better understood by asking:
**“Which symbolic, bodily, cute-contaminated, fake-sacred cartoon mutation rules are active
here?”**
That is the correct frame.
