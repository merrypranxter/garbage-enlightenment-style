# ANIMATION RECIPES
These recipes are for prompts involving motion, loops, metamorphosis, and animation logic in
the Garbage Enlightenment style.
## Core principle
Do not prompt this style like ordinary animation.
Prompt:
- transformation
- substitution
- contour continuity
- symbolic instability
- figure-pattern leakage
- fast meaningful intermediate states
Not:
- just movement
- just acting
- just locomotion
## RECIPE 1 — FAST MORPH LOOP
### Formula
A short looping animation in the Garbage Enlightenment style where [subject] rapidly morphs
through [state A], [state B], [state C], and several unstable intermediate states, using contour
inheritance, semantic substitution, rude black outlines, synthetic acid color, and cheap
dream-loop cadence; motion should feel like cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic translated into
symbolic cartoon metamorphosis
### Example
a floating mouth morphing into an eye, then into a little creature, then into a rainbow cavity, with
fast transition states, rude outline continuity, hot pink/acid yellow palette, and cheap trance-loop
timing
--## RECIPE 2 — FIGURE TO FIELD LOOP
### Formula
A looping animation where [figure] dissolves into [abstract field] and re-emerges, emphasizing
figure-pattern permeability, contour-band hallucination fields, symbolic body logic, low-fi
synthetic color, and meaningful half-formed states

### Example
a face dissolving into thermal contour bands and re-forming as a different face-creature, with
semantically active in-between states and slight fake revelation energy
--## RECIPE 3 — BODY GLYPH SUBSTITUTION LOOP
### Formula
A looping symbolic animation in the Garbage Enlightenment style where [eye / hand / mouth /
skull / tongue] keeps changing semantic role, becoming [other symbol or form], with graphic
simplicity, rude outlines, synthetic color, and unstable body-glyph logic
### Example
a hand turning into an eye icon, then into a portal ring, then into a mouth shape, with quick
substitutions and toy-occult charge
--## RECIPE 4 — CUTE CORRUPTION LOOP
### Formula
A sweet-but-wrong looping animation where [cute motif] repeatedly contaminates into [body
glyph or creature], keeping sticker-like charm while introducing symbolic wrongness, cheap
synthetic color, rude cartoon simplicity, and playful grossness
### Example
a rainbow and star cluster morphing into little faces and mouths and then back into candy
symbols, with a cute but body-weird emotional tone
--## RECIPE 5 — ABSTRACT SHADER LOOP
### Formula
A shader-like looping abstract animation in the Garbage Enlightenment style: contour-band
psychedelia, threshold blob emergence, liquid false topology, fast symbolic almost-states, and
cheap digital hallucination field behavior, as if the moving field is repeatedly trying to become an
eye, face, or creature
### Example
animated thermal rainbow topography with threshold blobs that repeatedly hint at eyes and
heads before melting back into contour soup

## Timing notes
Useful language:
- fast loop
- rapid symbolic substitution
- quick intermediate states
- trance-like recurrence
- cheap dream cadence
- low-fi looping metamorphosis
Avoid over-emphasizing:
- smooth cinematic motion
- realistic frame physics
- graceful choreography
## Final rule
A good Garbage Enlightenment animation prompt should describe:
- what changes into what
- how quickly
- what stays continuous
- what kind of symbolic pressure the loop carries
