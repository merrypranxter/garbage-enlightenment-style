# HOLY STICKER THEORY

This file focuses on a core conceptual truth inside Garbage Enlightenment:

**the holy sticker**

A holy sticker is one of the style’s most perfect compressed objects.

It is:
- portable
- symbolic
- cheap

- emotionally overcommitted
- body-contaminated
- decorative
- fake sacred
- modular
- slightly ridiculous
- weirdly convincing

## Core principle

A holy sticker should feel like:
- a devotional object
- a warning
- a fake relic
- a symbolic product
- a little image trying to be spiritually important
- a sticker that somehow acquired ritual significance

It should not feel like:
- premium branding
- reverent icon painting
- polished spiritual merch
- neutral graphic design

## WHY HOLY STICKERS MATTER

Holy sticker logic lets the style unify:
- sticker logic
- Cheap Holiness
- portable symbols
- fake revelation
- ritual bureaucracy
- Sweet Corruption
- body sign systems

It is one of the most useful internal theories in the whole repo.

## WHAT MAKES A STICKER “HOLY”

### 1. Framing
- halo
- ring
- contour aura
- central staging
- badge structure

### 2. Symbolic charge
- eye

- hand
- heart-mouth
- skull
- star-watcher
- fake sigil
- portal icon

### 3. Cheapness
- rude black outline
- flat synthetic color
- anti-luxury finish
- low-budget authority

### 4. Emotional over-importance
The sticker behaves like it matters too much.

## WHAT TO AVOID

### Too premium
Luxury kills the holy-sticker magic.

### Too ironic
Needs enough sincerity to still carry charge.

### Too empty
Must still feel like a symbol, not just a decoration.
