# NAMING OPTIONS

This file lists possible names, subtitles, and slugs for the style and repo.

The goal is not to decide once and for all here.
The goal is to preserve the useful naming possibilities.

## CURRENT STRONG CANDIDATE

### Main title
**Garbage Enlightenment**

### Current subtitle
**Sweet Corruption Acid Cartoon Surrealism**

Why it works:
- “Garbage” keeps it low-fi, bodily, rude, anti-luxury
- “Enlightenment” keeps the fake-sacred / revelation / LSD-adjacent symbolic charge
- “Sweet Corruption” captures the cute ↔ weird crossover
- “Acid Cartoon Surrealism” keeps the broad formal territory visible

- hallucinatory logic

- Sweet Corruption

## FINAL RULE

A good title for this style should suggest:
- mutation
- symbolic charge
- low-fi weirdness
- sweet/gross crossover
- fake revelation or hallucinatory intelligence

It should not sound:
- too corporate
- too generic
- too medical
- too literal
- too spiritually sincere

That would miss the whole dirty little magic trick.
