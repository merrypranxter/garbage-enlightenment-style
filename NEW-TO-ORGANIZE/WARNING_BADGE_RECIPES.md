# WARNING BADGE RECIPES

This file contains recipes for making warning-badge imagery in Garbage Enlightenment.

Warning badges are useful because they combine:
- symbol clarity
- sticker portability
- fake authority
- body glyph logic
- ritual bureaucracy
- Soft Menace
- Cheap Holiness

A warning badge is stronger than a generic symbol because it feels like:
- it has a job
- it belongs to a system
- it is trying to inform, caution, or bless
- it may not fully understand its own instructions

## Core principle

A Garbage Enlightenment warning badge should feel like:
- a little official
- a little wrong
- body-made

- emotionally overcommitted
- visually blunt
- able to work on a sticker sheet, poster, patch, or emblem board

It should not feel like:
- modern UI safety signage
- polished corporate iconography
- luxury occult branding
- generic spooky merch

## RECIPE 1 — BODY WARNING BADGE

### Formula
Choose:
- eye
- mouth
- hand
- skull
- heart-mouth hybrid
- blob glyph

Put it inside:
- circle
- triangle

- soft badge shape
- seal outline
- halo frame

Then add:
- one contaminant
- one support clutter family
- one anti-polish cue

### Example
A circular warning badge with an eye-mouth hybrid in the center, rude black outline, acid yellow
and weird peach palette, little stars around the edge, and a broken halo ring to fake authority
while keeping it weird

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Warning badges become more Garbage Enlightenment-specific when:
- a simple sign feels too emotionally loaded
- a cute symbol becomes a caution glyph
- the badge implies multiple symbolic jobs at once
- the warning feels both ridiculous and weirdly valid

That is a compact form of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic.
