# MOCK ESOTERICA
Garbage Enlightenment often borrows from occult, mystical, paranoid, conspiratorial, visionary,
and pseudo-sacred visual language.
But it does not usually treat those systems with full solemn reverence.
Instead, it tends to transform them into:
- cartoon theater
- parody revelation
- fake-serious symbolism
- bodily ritual nonsense
- stupid cosmic signage
- dream propaganda
- prankster mysticism

## Core principle
Esoteric imagery in this style should often feel:
- charged
- iconic
- weirdly potent
- visually convincing enough to register
but also:
- unserious
- unstable
- absurd
- bodily
- contaminated
- low-fi
- fake-profound
- cartoon-warped
## The key emotional target
Not:
- sacred grandeur
- polished visionary revelation
- serious occult orthodoxy
More like:
- “this looks like it means something, but it’s also clearly a little full of shit”
- “the symbol is trying to look wise while growing a mouth”
- “the universe is broadcasting nonsense through a cheap cartoon icon”
## Common symbol families
--## 1. ALL-SEEING EYES
The eye is probably the strongest mock-esoteric symbol in the repo.
### Possible functions
- surveillance
- revelation
- false revelation
- cosmic witnessing

- paranoia
- logo-ification of consciousness
- divine joke
- fake authority
- portal emblem
### Good use
- isolated central icon
- embedded in pyramids or halos
- repeated in patterns
- nested inside mouths, hearts, clouds, hands, rainbows
- floating above symbolic tableaux
--## 2. PYRAMIDS / TRIANGLES
Triangles and pyramid forms instantly activate:
- Illuminati associations
- mystic geometry
- warning-sign logic
- logo energy
- fake hidden-order vibes
### Good use
- eye-in-pyramid
- crude pyramid emblems
- glowing but cheap triangles
- pyramid as character
- pyramid as joke relic
- soft, blobby, or mutated pyramid shapes
### Important note
These should not drift into slick conspiracy merch aesthetics.
Keep them a little rude, synthetic, or weird.
--## 3. PORTALS / HALOS / RINGS
Circular or elliptical rings can function as:
- aura
- gateway
- tunnel

- loop device
- ritual frame
- fake ascension symbol
- animation track for emergence
### Good use
- around eyes
- behind heads
- around hands
- as entry points into abstract fields
- as looping animation motifs
- as decorative cosmic nonsense
These are especially useful when bridging figure and pattern.
--## 4. HAND-SIGN SERIOUSNESS
Hands can be made mock-esoteric very easily.
### Possible functions
- blessing gesture
- warning gesture
- palmistry parody
- mystic sign
- false ritual icon
- vague “energy” image
- spiritual-poster bullshit made strange
### Best use
Keep the hand simple, centered, emblematic, and a little too solemn for what it actually is.
That tension is good.
--## 5. SIGIL-LIKE SHAPES
The repo can include invented symbolic forms that resemble:
- sigils
- seals
- ritual diagrams
- mystical scripts

- occult logos
- sacred geometry-adjacent signs
But in this style they should often remain:
- slightly dumb
- bodily
- asymmetrical
- hand-done
- unstable
- cartoon-fit
Do not overcomplicate them into pristine ceremonial diagrams unless specifically needed.
--## 6. COSMIC WARNING IMAGERY
This style often likes images that feel like:
- fake transmissions
- secret warnings
- esoteric signage
- dangerous stickers
- spiritual hazard labels
- revelation memes
This is especially good when mixed with:
- eyes
- skulls
- mouths
- cute motifs
- rainbow abstractions
## Valid moods of mock esoterica
### 1. Cartoon ritual
Looks like a sacred image, but simplified into dumb graphic icon language.
### 2. Paranoid symbolism
Feels watchful, surveillance-heavy, conspiratorial, but still stylized and weird.
### 3. Fake transcendence
Looks like revelation is occurring, but it’s clearly cartoon-mutant revelation.
### 4. Cosmic joke

The symbol appears serious while the image undermines it.
### 5. Sweet cult energy
Cute motifs coexist with secret-symbol theater.
## Good tensions
Mock esoterica becomes especially strong when fused with:
- bodily glyphs
- childish sweetness
- rude linework
- cheap rainbow abstraction
- pathetic little creatures
- dreamlike mutation
- looping animation
Examples:
- pyramid with a mouth
- all-seeing eye inside a rainbow
- halo around a gross hand
- ritual diagram made from heads
- sigil that looks half-organic
- fake saint image with cartoon mutation
- portal eye birthing cute freaks
## What to avoid
### Too solemn
- noble sacred geometry
- spiritually reverent illustration
- serious ceremonial occult aesthetics
- over-beautified visionary mysticism
### Too polished
- luxury occult branding
- sleek digital logo mysticism
- crisp merch-core Illuminati graphics
### Too generic
- random occult symbols pasted in with no mutation or emotional integration
## Prompting correction rules
If mock-esoteric imagery gets too serious:

- add bodily mutation
- reduce symmetry
- add one ridiculous detail
- make the linework cheaper
- introduce cute contamination
- turn one symbol into a creature or mouth
If it gets too dumb and loses symbolic charge:
- simplify composition
- strengthen central icon
- use more iconic staging
- add aura/ring/pyramid/eye hierarchy
- let the symbol hold the frame more strongly
## Final rule
Mock esoterica should feel like:
**a symbol trying very hard to be profound while the rest of the image keeps exposing it as
weird cartoon meat theater**
That tension is essential.
