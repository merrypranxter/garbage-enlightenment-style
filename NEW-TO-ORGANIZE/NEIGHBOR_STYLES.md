# NEIGHBOR STYLES

This file lists neighboring styles that Garbage Enlightenment may touch, borrow from, or be
confused with.

These styles are not enemies.
They are nearby territories.
But this repo needs to know where the border lines are.

## 1. PSYCHEDELIC CARTOON SURREALISM

### Why it is nearby
- surreal mutation
- synthetic color
- altered-state implication
- cartoon logic
- symbolic weirdness

### Where Garbage Enlightenment differs
- more body glyphs
- more fake esoterica
- more cheapness
- more sweet contamination
- more anti-polish
- more LSD-logic emphasis in metamorphic structure

---

## RELATION TO LSD-HALLUCINATION AESTHETICS

Garbage Enlightenment can be placed near certain broad “psychedelic hallucination” aesthetics,
but it is more precise than that.

It resembles **cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic** in:
- rapid symbolic reassignment
- pattern/figure crossover
- significance overload
- emotional over-meaning
- recursive visual implication
- fast or implied metamorphosis

But unlike many psychedelic styles, it filters those through:
- crude cartoon compression
- body glyph systems

- mock esoterica
- Sweet Corruption
- anti-polish cheapness

## FINAL RULE

Neighbor styles are useful reference zones.
But Garbage Enlightenment becomes itself only when the full mutant stack is active.

Without that, it drifts next door and starts paying the wrong rent.
