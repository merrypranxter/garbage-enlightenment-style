# STYLE PILLARS
These are the core pillars of Garbage Enlightenment.
If too many of these disappear, the style drifts into something else.
--## 1. CARTOON PRIMITIVISM

The drawing language should remain simple, direct, rude, and somewhat unrefined.
### Includes
- flat shapes
- direct outlines
- simplified anatomy
- anti-polish drawing energy
- lo-fi digital or handmade-crude feel
- anti-corporate visual bluntness
### Does not require
- technical incompetence
- random sloppiness
- ugly-for-no-reason chaos
The point is not “bad drawing.”
The point is **deliberately unpretentious graphic directness**.
--## 2. BODILY GLYPHS
Body parts are not just anatomy.
They are symbolic units.
### Core glyph forms
- eyes
- mouths
- lips
- tongues
- teeth
- hands
- ears
- skulls
- detached heads
- feet
- organ-ish blobs
These can function as:
- emblems
- icons
- totems
- repeating motifs

- portals
- jokes
- warnings
- decorative elements
- mutated creature components
--## 3. MOCK ESOTERICA
Occult, mystical, conspiratorial, visionary, or pseudo-sacred imagery is allowed, but it should
rarely be fully solemn.
### Common material
- all-seeing eyes
- pyramids
- portals
- cosmic rings
- sigil-like arrangements
- spiritual-hand motifs
- false ritual objects
- fake enlightenment graphics
- secret-symbol theater
### Key attitude
This imagery may be eerie or striking, but it is often:
- parodic
- dumb
- bodily
- playful
- ridiculous
- cartoon-corrupted
- fake-profound on purpose
--## 4. MUTANT METAMORPHOSIS
Forms should feel evolutionarily unstable.
### Common behaviors
- fusing
- splitting
- stretching

- revealing hidden identities
- growing extra parts
- leaking into adjacent symbolic forms
- becoming one another without clean boundaries
This applies to both still imagery and animation.
Even still images should often imply transformability.
--## 5. NATIVE ACID COLOR
The palette should emerge from the style’s own ecosystem first.
### Common palette behaviors
- synthetic primaries and secondaries
- hot pinks
- acid yellows
- weird reds
- dirty peaches
- toxic greens
- candy blues
- chemical cyan
- flat black outlines
- occasional deadened neutral ground
### Important note
Do not force unrelated personal palette preferences onto the base style definition.
Optional palettes can be added later as modules.
--## 6. CHEAP HALLUCINATION ABSTRACTION
The style includes a strong abstract subsystem.
### Abstract forms may include
- contour-band psychedelia
- oil-slick fields
- false topographies
- liquid rainbow distortion
- warped heatmap space
- shader-like motion fields
- plasma blobs

- posterized melt regions
These abstractions are not separate from the figurative work.
They are part of the same style universe.
--## 7. SWEET CORRUPTION
Cute visual language is allowed and often essential.
### Includes
- stars
- hearts
- rainbows
- clouds
- dolphins
- unicorn-ish motifs
- playful critters
- sticker forms
- toy-like symbols
- childlike decorative sweetness
### But
This sweetness should remain vulnerable to:
- mutation
- bodily contamination
- symbolic weirdness
- ironic charge
- occult parody
- grossification
- dream instability
This is not pristine cuteness.
This is sweetness that has been touched by weirdness.
--## 8. GOOFY COSMIC DREAD
The style can be eerie, but it should not usually become grand and solemn.
### Tone target
- absurd menace

- stupid revelation
- prankster unease
- synthetic mysticism
- cartoon paranoia
- dream-logic weirdness
- fake seriousness
- funny existential wrongness
This pillar protects the style from drifting into:
- grimdark horror
- serious occult art
- noble visionary art
- overdramatic symbolic fantasy
--## 9. INTERMEDIATE STATE MEANING
Half-formed states matter.
### In practice
- in-between morph frames are meaningful
- transitional forms are not junk
- almost-recognizable states are aesthetically important
- semantic ambiguity is part of the style
- an unfinished mutation may be more “correct” than a final stabilized pose
This is crucial for both animation and prompting.
--## 10. FIGURE–PATTERN PERMEABILITY
Figures and abstract fields must be allowed to bleed into each other.
### Examples
- an eye emerges from a contour field
- a face melts into rainbow bands
- a creature dissolves into plasma
- a body part becomes a repeating graphic unit
- a symbol becomes a creature
- a creature becomes decoration
This is one of the most important structural laws in the whole repo.

--## Pillar summary
Garbage Enlightenment depends on the coexistence of:
- rude simplification
- bodily symbolism
- parody-esoteric imagery
- transformability
- synthetic color
- cheap abstraction
- sweet contamination
- comic unease
- meaningful in-betweens
- figure-pattern leakage
Break too many of those and the style becomes some other bastard.
