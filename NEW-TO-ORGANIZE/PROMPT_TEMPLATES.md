# PROMPT TEMPLATES
These templates are designed to preserve the logic of Garbage Enlightenment across:
- still images
- animation prompts
- shader prompts
- creature generation
- symbol design
- figure–pattern hybrids
They emphasize:
- body glyphs
- sweet corruption
- mock esoterica
- rude cartoon flatness
- synthetic acid palette logic
- metamorphic instability

- abstract hallucination fields
- cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic where relevant
--## TEMPLATE 1 — CORE STILL IMAGE
A flat, low-fi mutant cartoon image in the **Garbage Enlightenment / Sweet Corruption Acid
Cartoon Surrealism** style, featuring [main subject], drawn with rude black outlines, simplified
graphic forms, synthetic acid color, bodily glyph logic, and symbolic instability; include [body
glyphs], [cute motifs], and [mock-esoteric elements], with a tone that balances [cute / gross /
ominous / silly / fake-sacred]; the composition should feel like a symbolic sticker-sheet
revelation, slightly cheap, slightly hallucinatory, and ready to morph into another form; avoid
polished realism, corporate vector cleanliness, solemn mysticism, or generic trippy rainbow slop.
--## TEMPLATE 2 — CREATURE PORTRAIT
A weird little creature portrait in the **Garbage Enlightenment** style, designed as a pathetic
cosmic goblin / cute-corrupted mutant being, with simplified anatomy, awkward limbs, a face
built from [eyes / mouths / teeth / tongues / ears], graphic black outlines, body-part symbolism,
and synthetic candy-toxic color; make it feel lovable, bodily, wrong, and slightly fake-profound,
as if it were born from a body glyph, a joke, and a cartoonized LSD-hallucination all at once.
--## TEMPLATE 3 — MOCK ESOTERIC ICON
A fake-sacred cartoon symbol in the **Garbage Enlightenment** style: [eye / hand / pyramid /
portal / sigil] rendered as a low-fi, rude, acid-colored emblem with bodily contamination, graphic
flatness, and symbolic over-meaning; make it feel charged, eerie, and ridiculous at the same
time, like a cosmic warning sticker from a broken revelation pamphlet; include simplified contour
logic, black outlines, and at least one element of cute corruption or mutation.
--## TEMPLATE 4 — SWEET CORRUPTION IMAGE
A sweet-but-wrong psychedelic cartoon scene in the **Garbage Enlightenment** style,
combining [stars / hearts / rainbows / dolphins / unicorn-ish motifs / cute creatures] with [eyes /
mouths / teeth / body blobs / occult parody symbols], using rude outlined forms, synthetic
cartoon color, playful asymmetry, and slight mutational contamination; the image should feel
dreamy, silly, and a little bodily, never pristine, never fully innocent.

--## TEMPLATE 5 — ABSTRACT HALLUCINATION FIELD
An abstract hallucination field in the **Garbage Enlightenment** style: contour-band

--## TEMPLATE 6 — FIGURE–PATTERN HYBRID
A figure dissolving into an abstract hallucination field in the **Garbage Enlightenment** style,
where [face / creature / hand / eye / skull] emerges from and leaks back into contour bands,
rainbow topographies, plasma blobs, and symbolic decorative units; emphasize figure–pattern
permeability, body glyph logic, rude outlines, meaningful in-between states, and cartoonized
LSD-hallucination metamorphosis.
--## TEMPLATE 7 — ANIMATION / LOOP PROMPT
A fast-looping mutant cartoon animation in the **Garbage Enlightenment** style, built around
continuous symbolic metamorphosis rather than normal action: [subject] should rapidly morph
through [state A], [state B], [state C], and unstable intermediate forms, with contour inheritance,
semantic substitution, figure–pattern leakage, and cheap dream-loop cadence; the motion
should feel like cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic translated into rude graphic symbols, where
one form keeps discovering other forms hidden inside itself.
--## TEMPLATE 8 — BODY GLYPH SHEET
A body-glyph symbol sheet in the **Garbage Enlightenment** style, featuring floating [eyes /
mouths / hands / tongues / teeth / skulls / ears / detached heads] treated as emblems, stickers,
warnings, portals, and decorative icons; use black outlines, simple flat shapes, symbolic
weirdness, synthetic color, and a mood that moves between cute absurdity, gross-funny body

## Modular phrase bank
### Style anchors
- Garbage Enlightenment
- Sweet Corruption Acid Cartoon Surrealism
- rude black outlines
- low-fi mutant cartoon
- symbolic body weirdness
- fake-sacred sticker logic
- cartoon grotesque with cute contamination
- cheap hallucination field
- prankster occult energy
### Motion anchors
- continuous symbolic metamorphosis
- contour inheritance
- fast meaningful in-between states
- figure–pattern leakage
- identity-sliding morphs
- cheap dream-loop cadence
- semantic substitution
- unstable symbolic emergence
### LSD relation anchors
Use these carefully:
- cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic

- symbolic over-meaning
- rapid identity shifts
- one form containing another
- hallucination compressed into rude graphic symbols
- over-significant visual mutation
- psychedelic morph logic without polished visionary-art drift
### Anti-drift phrases
- avoid polished realism
- avoid solemn mystical grandeur
- avoid corporate clean vector illustration
- avoid generic trippy rainbow slop
- avoid pristine kawaii sweetness
- avoid grim realistic body horror
- avoid elegant cosmic beauty
## Final rule
A good prompt for this style should not just say:
“make it weird.”
It should specify:
- **what kind of weird**
- **what symbolic units matter**
- **what tonal mixture is active**
- **how the image should move or imply movement**
- **what kinds of drift to avoid**
That is how the style keeps its teeth.
