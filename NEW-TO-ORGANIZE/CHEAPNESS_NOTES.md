# CHEAPNESS NOTES

This file explains the role of cheapness in Garbage Enlightenment.

Cheapness here does not mean:

- bad taste in a dismissive sense
- low effort
- incompetence
- accidental ugliness

It means:
- anti-luxury
- anti-corporate
- anti-overfinished
- anti-noble
- blunt
- synthetic
- low-rent sacredness
- bootleg symbolic intensity

## Core principle

Cheapness is one of the style’s strongest life sources.

Without it, the repo often drifts into:
- polished surrealism
- clean occult design
- premium sticker art
- visionary prettiness

- cool detached weirdness

Cheapness keeps the style:
- alive
- rude
- bodily
- funny
- emotionally overcommitted
- unstable

## WHAT CHEAPNESS LOOKS LIKE

### Line cheapness
- rude outlines
- anti-perfect contour
- slight wobble
- anti-logo polish
- hand-wrong force

### Color cheapness
- synthetic brights
- rude clashes
- fake candy chroma
- toy-plastic pressure

- thermal weirdness
- body colors interrupting sweetness

### Symbol cheapness
- fake sacred icons that look low-budget
- revelation imagery that feels bootleg
- warnings that are too emotional
- little creatures that look underqualified

### Surface cheapness
- dither
- signal grit
- screen-like friction
- slight compression logic
- anti-luxury finish

## EMOTIONAL CHEAPNESS

This is a huge part of it.

The style often benefits from:
- sincerity without prestige
- over-significance without authority
- creaturehood without grandeur

- revelation without beauty standards
- cuteness without polish
- weirdness without self-protective coolness

That is emotional cheapness, and it matters.

## WHAT CHEAPNESS IS NOT

Cheapness is not:
- random ugliness
- meaningless mess
- total illegibility
- no design intelligence
- no symbolic hierarchy

It still needs:
- readability
- motif intelligence
- form control
- composition pressure
- intentionality

## HOW TO ADD CHEAPNESS

If the output feels too premium:
- flatten it
- roughen the line
- simplify the form
- make one decision more awkward
- use more synthetic color
- add a sticker-like framing cue
- reduce painterly finish
- make the sacred symbol a little embarrassing

## CHEAPNESS + LSD-LOGIC

The style’s LSD-adjacent side gets much stronger when it is filtered through cheapness.

Why:
Literal visionary grandeur tends to smooth and beautify hallucinatory logic.

Cheapness keeps it:
- rude
- compressed
- symbolic
- body-haunted
- a little fake
- emotionally unstable

That is exactly what Garbage Enlightenment wants.

## FINAL RULE

Cheapness in this style should feel like:
**a counterfeit revelation printed by a nervous little cartoon body that really, really wanted you to
see it**
