# FAILURE MODES
This document lists the most common ways Garbage Enlightenment can fail, flatten, or drift into
lesser bastard forms.
These are not moral failures.
They are style failures.
## 1. GENERIC TRIPPY MUSH
### What happens
The image becomes vaguely psychedelic but loses all specific identity.
### Symptoms
- rainbow everywhere
- no body-symbol intelligence
- no motif control
- no emotional friction
- no readable glyphs
- no sense of fake revelation or sweet corruption
### Why it fails
Psychedelia alone is not the style.

### Fix
Reintroduce:
- eyes
- mouths
- hands
- skulls
- body weirdness
- rude outlines
- clear symbolic subject
- contour discipline
--## 2. POLISHED SURREAL ILLUSTRATION DRIFT
### What happens
The work gets too beautiful, too elegant, too rendered.
### Symptoms
- refined shading
- tasteful composition
- luxury texture
- polished lighting
- smooth visual sophistication
- no cheapness
### Why it fails
Garbage Enlightenment needs rude compression and anti-luxury energy.
### Fix
Flatten it.
Cheapen it.
Outline it.
Make it more symbolic and less painterly.
--## 3. PRISTINE CUTE DRIFT
### What happens
The work becomes adorable but toothless.
### Symptoms

- too clean
- too sweet
- too mascot-like
- no mutation
- no contamination
- no body-symbol weirdness
### Why it fails
Sweetness is only powerful here when it is destabilized.
### Fix
Add:
- bodily glyphs
- asymmetry
- semantic wrongness
- fake occult signs
- slightly gross accents
--## 4. GRIM HORROR DRIFT
### What happens
The work becomes oppressive, gorey, or too serious.
### Symptoms
- realistic viscera
- cruelty-first tone
- joyless monsters
- severe darkness
- no playfulness
- no sticker logic
### Why it fails
The style prefers symbolic and comic-grotesque wrongness over fully grim horror.
### Fix
Simplify.
Cartoonize.
Reintroduce:
- stars
- hearts
- fake revelation
- awkward cuteness

- gross-funny energy
--## 5. OCCULT MERCH DRIFT
### What happens
The work starts looking like sleek conspiracy merch, tattoo flash, or designer occult branding.
### Symptoms
- ultra-clean eye triangle
- black-and-gold sacred coolness
- crisp symmetrical sigils
- no bodily interference
- no awkwardness
### Why it fails
The symbol has become too authoritative and too cool.
### Fix
Contaminate the symbol:
- add body weirdness
- break symmetry
- roughen edges
- add cute sabotage
- make it a little embarrassing
--## 6. EMPTY ABSTRACTION DRIFT
### What happens
The abstract field becomes pretty wallpaper with no symbolic stakes.
### Symptoms
- smooth gradients
- generic flow
- no emergence pressure
- no body hints
- no glyph potential
- no weird semantic resonance
### Why it fails
In this repo, abstraction should feel like almost-meaning.

### Fix
Add:
- latent face logic
- eye emergence
- mouth cavities
- contour thresholds
- body-haunted pattern behavior
--## 7. TOO COOL DRIFT
### What happens
The image gets stylish in a dead way.
### Symptoms
- detached confidence
- sleek underground branding energy
- tasteful darkness
- too much design literacy and not enough freak instability
### Why it fails
Garbage Enlightenment benefits from:
- awkwardness
- vulnerability
- pathetic creaturehood
- symbolic embarrassment
- body stupidity
### Fix
Make it softer, stranger, or more bodily.
Humiliate the coolness slightly.
--## 8. MOTION WITHOUT METAMORPHOSIS
### What happens
Animation moves, but nothing actually changes meaning.
### Symptoms
- normal walk cycle
- smooth floating motion

- idle bobbing
- simple camera movement
- abstract motion with no symbolic emergence
### Why it fails
This style’s motion identity comes from symbolic substitution and morph continuity.
### Fix
Add:
- fast in-between states
- contour inheritance
- identity-sliding changes
- figure-pattern transitions
- one thing becoming another
--## 9. LSD NAME-CHECK WITHOUT STRUCTURAL UNDERSTANDING
### What happens
Someone says “LSD style” but only adds rainbow noise and eyeballs.
### Symptoms
- lazy trippy clichés
- no actual visual substitution logic
- no significance overload
- no recursive implication
- no mutational structure
- no contour intelligence
### Why it fails
The style’s relation to LSD is structural, not just decorative.
### Fix
Focus on:
- symbolic compression
- unstable identities
- pattern/figure crossover
- rapid metamorphosis
- over-meaning
- cartoonized hallucination logic
---

## 10. TOO STABLE
### What happens
The image settles down too much.
### Symptoms
- every form fully resolved
- no implication of change
- no emotional wobble
- no second reading
- no hidden alternate form
### Why it fails
Even still images should feel like paused mutation.
### Fix
Add:
- asymmetry
- embedded secondary form
- contour tension
- symbolic ambiguity
- partial emergence
- suggestive overlaps
## Final rule
The biggest style failure is not ugliness.
It is **stability without weird pressure**.
If the image feels too settled, too correct, too self-contained, or too sure of itself, something has
gone wrong.
