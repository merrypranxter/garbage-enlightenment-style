# REVELATION POSTER NOTES

This file focuses specifically on revelation-poster logic in Garbage Enlightenment.

A revelation poster is an image that feels like:
- an announcement
- a sermon
- a symbolic warning
- a fake sacred advertisement
- a cheap holy poster
- a cosmic notice
- a body-sign proclamation

It is one of the strongest compositional formats in the repo.

## Core principle

A revelation poster should feel:

- over-significant
- graphic
- body-contaminated
- weirdly official
- low-budget holy
- rude
- poster-readable
- emotionally unstable

It should not feel like:
- polished spiritual poster art
- mystical luxury decor
- solemn ceremonial imagery
- tasteful visionary advertising

## CORE REVELATION POSTER STRUCTURE

A strong revelation poster often includes:

### 1. One dominant icon
- eye
- hand
- face
- skull

- heart-mouth hybrid
- portal symbol
- body goblin emblem

### 2. Fake sacred support
- halo
- rings
- contour aura
- symbolic framing
- little sigils

### 3. Secondary contamination
- stars
- hearts
- extra eyes
- mouths
- little symbols
- body weirdness

### 4. Flat symbolic stage
- void background
- contour-band field
- decorative clutter
- minimal environment

## TEXTURE / SURFACE NOTES

A revelation poster often benefits from:
- low-fi print feeling
- slight cheapness
- rude color
- anti-polish flatness
- sticker/emblem readability

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Revelation posters become especially Garbage Enlightenment-specific when:
- the image feels too meaningful for what it is
- simple symbols behave like they are transmitting more than they should
- body and sacred roles contaminate each other
- poster clarity hides deeper symbolic instability

This is one of the clearest static forms of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic.
