# ICON STAGING NOTES

This file focuses on how to stage icons, glyphs, and symbolic central forms inside Garbage
Enlightenment.

Icons matter a lot in this style because the whole thing often lives or dies on whether a symbol
feels:
- strong
- weird

- legible
- charged
- unstable
- slightly fake-sacred
- slightly body-haunted

## Core principle

An icon in Garbage Enlightenment should usually feel like:
- a sticker
- a warning sign
- a ritual image
- a cheap holy emblem
- a body part pretending to be a logo
- a logo pretending to be a creature
- a creature compressed into a symbolic sign

## WHY ICON STAGING MATTERS

This style often uses:
- floating body glyphs
- fake revelation symbols
- little emblem creatures
- poster-like icon panels

- symbol sheets

If the staging is weak, the image becomes:
- vague
- mushy
- overdecorated
- too cute
- too empty
- too genericly weird

Good icon staging keeps the image:
- focused
- readable
- symbolic
- ready to mutate

## PRIMARY ICON STAGING MODES

---

## 1. CENTRAL ICON MODE

### Description
One large symbol occupies the compositional center and holds the frame.

### Best subjects
- eye
- hand
- face
- skull
- pyramid
- portal
- heart-mouth hybrid
- creature emblem

### Why it works
- strong poster energy
- easy symbolic readability
- good for fake revelation
- easy to contaminate with cute or bodily elements
- easy to animate later

### Good support elements
- halo rings
- stars
- contour fields
- small repeated motifs
- decorative corners

---

## 2. ICON WITH ORBITING CLUTTER

### Description
A central icon remains dominant, but small surrounding motifs orbit or contaminate it.

### Good orbiters
- stars
- eyes
- little mouths
- bubbles
- glyph marks
- little heads
- decorative symbols

### Why it works
- central clarity stays intact
- style gets to add weird symbolic weather
- good for Sweet Corruption and Mock Revelation

---

## 3. BADGE / STICKER PANEL MODE

### Description
The icon is contained in a self-sufficient emblem unit that could work like a sticker, badge, or
patch.

### Best for
- prompts
- motif sheets
- shirt graphics
- symbols
- fake cult logos
- body glyph collections

### Good traits
- blunt silhouette
- contained composition
- strong outline
- limited but intense accent field

---

## 4. FLOATING GLYPH MODE

### Description

Icons are detached and floating in a loose field rather than anchored in one center.

### Best for
- sticker-sheet compositions
- body glyph studies
- symbol swarms
- playful modular pages

### Danger
Can become too scattered or too decorative with no hierarchy.

### Fix
Make at least one glyph more dominant.

## ICON SIZE LOGIC

### Too small
Loses charge and gets swallowed by clutter.

### Too large
Can work great, but needs enough contour intelligence or contamination to stay alive.

### Best range
Usually large enough to:
- read immediately
- tolerate contamination
- imply mutational potential

### Hybrid icon
The strongest category.
Example:
- heart with teeth
- star with eye
- hand with mouth
- skull with rainbow halo

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Icon staging becomes more Garbage Enlightenment-specific when the icon feels:
- symbolically overloaded
- like it means several things at once
- visually simple but semantically unstable
- poised to change identity if stared at too long

That is one of the most useful ways to encode cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic in static
compositions.
