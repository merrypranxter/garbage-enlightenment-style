# NEGATIVE DRIFT CONTROLS
This file provides practical negative-control language for prompts, systems, and style
refinement.
These are not meant to sterilize the work.
They are meant to stop very specific wrong turns.

## Core principle
Do not use negatives to make the image boring or over-sanitized.
Use them to prevent:
- polish drift
- tonal drift
- generic trippy drift
- cute-purity drift
- solemn occult drift
- grim horror drift
## MASTER NEGATIVE BLOCK
Avoid:
- polished realism
- elegant surreal painting
- corporate clean vector illustration
- pristine kawaii sweetness
- solemn mystical grandeur
- sleek occult branding
- generic rainbow-fractal slop
- majestic spiritual aura art
- grim realistic gore
- tasteful contemporary editorial illustration
- over-rendered psychedelic mush
- luxury pop surrealism
- stable mascot coherence
- sterile graphic minimalism
## CATEGORY NEGATIVES
### Anti-polish
- no polished finish
- no luxury rendering
- no sleek gradients
- no elegant refinement
- no premium design sheen
### Anti-corporate
- no startup illustration energy
- no editorial flat-design cleanliness
- no friendly corporate blob aesthetic

- no sanitized playful branding
### Anti-cute drift
- no pristine kawaii polish
- no innocent mascot perfection
- no harmless sticker sweetness
- no pure fantasy-animal poster energy
### Anti-occult drift
- no solemn sacred geometry
- no reverent mystical iconography
- no crisp black-and-gold occult branding
- no ceremonial seriousness played straight
### Anti-horror drift
- no realistic gore
- no clinical flesh realism
- no oppressive grimdark monster tone
- no cruelty-first horror imagery
### Anti-generic psychedelic drift
- no default fractal wallpaper
- no generic cosmic rainbow mush
- no mandala drift
- no polished visionary tunnel art
- no stock “trippy” aesthetics
## CONDITIONAL NEGATIVE BLOCKS
### If image is too cute
Avoid:
- perfect symmetry
- harmless charm
- over-clean decorative sweetness
- polished toy mascot finish
Add instead:
- asymmetry
- bodily glyphs
- symbolic wrongness
- contamination
### If image is too dark
Avoid:

- gore realism
- grim shadow dominance
- oppressive menace
- demon-grandeur seriousness
Add instead:
- stars
- sweet motifs
- toy logic
- fake revelation absurdity
### If image is too abstract
Avoid:
- empty wallpaper abstraction
- meaningless color drift
- detached procedural beauty
Add instead:
- body-glyph emergence
- symbolic pressure
- latent figure logic
### If image is too serious
Avoid:
- sacred nobility
- profound stillness
- ritual austerity
- too much authority
Add instead:
- awkwardness
- cheapness
- bodily mutation
- one stupid or embarrassing symbolic disruption

## FINAL RULE
Negative drift controls should protect the freak.
They should not declaw it.
Do not use negatives to make the image safe.
Use them to keep it specific.
