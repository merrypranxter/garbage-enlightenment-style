# DECORATIVE CLUTTER NOTES

This file explains decorative clutter in Garbage Enlightenment.

Decorative clutter matters because the style often uses:
- little stars
- hearts
- bubbles
- glyph marks
- mini eyes
- sparkles
- fake sigils
- repeated mini symbols

But the clutter must stay alive.
It cannot become dead ornament.

## Core principle

Decorative clutter should:
- support the main symbol

- increase emotional tone
- add contamination
- create pressure
- imply swarm logic
- suggest fake ritual insistence
- help the image feel over-significant

It should not:
- become elegant filigree
- act as meaningless filler
- flatten the hierarchy
- turn the image into wallpaper

## GOOD CLUTTER QUALITIES

- related to the main symbol family
- tonally supportive

- varied but not random
- hierarchically secondary
- slightly alive
- helping the image feel contaminated or charged

## BAD CLUTTER QUALITIES

- pure filler
- elegant ornament
- unearned symmetry
- too much equal-weight noise
- decoration with no symbolic or tonal role

## CLUTTER + LSD-LOGIC

Decorative clutter becomes stronger when it creates:
- over-meaning

- swarm consciousness
- emotional pressure
- the feeling that small symbols are echoing the big symbol
- pattern consciousness
- distributed face pressure

That makes clutter feel alive rather than merely cute.
