# BAD MASCOT FAILURES

This file focuses on one particular failure mode in Garbage Enlightenment:

the image becomes a **bad mascot** in the wrong way.

Important distinction:
the style absolutely can use mascot-like energy.
In fact, little creatures, stickers, and emblem beings are a huge part of it.

But there is a difference between:
- weird mascot charm
and
- dead mascot failure

## Core principle

A good Garbage Enlightenment mascot-like being feels:

- contaminated
- awkward
- body-symbolic
- emotionally unstable
- fake-significant
- slightly pathetic
- low-fi
- rude
- not too polished

A bad mascot failure feels:
- overclean
- safe
- generic
- brand-ready
- coherent in a dead way
- too cute
- too market-tested

## MAIN BAD MASCOT FAILURE TYPES

---

## 1. TOO CLEANLY CUTE

### Symptoms
- rounded harmless proportions
- polished sweetness
- no contamination
- no symbolic weirdness
- no body tension

### Why it fails
Sweetness is not enough.
The style needs wrongness.

---

## 2. TOO COHERENT

### Symptoms
- well-designed character sheet energy
- species consistency
- good anatomy in the wrong way
- no mutational instability
- no alternate readings

### Why it fails

The creature becomes too finished.

---

## 3. TOO BRANDABLE

### Symptoms
- looks like merch mascot
- simplified in a corporate way
- emotionally safe
- could represent a startup or consumer product

### Why it fails
Garbage Enlightenment needs anti-corporate weirdness.

---

## 4. TOO COOL

### Symptoms
- confident attitude
- design swagger
- edgy but polished
- no vulnerability

- no symbolic embarrassment

### Why it fails
The best creatures here are slightly underqualified for being.

---

## 5. TOO PURELY “SPOOKY CUTE”

### Symptoms
- goth-cute cliché
- spooky merch energy
- cute skull with no weird symbolic pressure
- easy Hot Topic drift

### Why it fails
The repo wants stranger and more unstable tone than that.

## HOW TO REPAIR BAD MASCOT DRIFT

### Add body glyph weirdness
- bigger eye
- weirder mouth
- body-sign contamination

### Add asymmetry
- off-center feature
- one side more wrong
- unstable contour

### Add emotional instability
- tender + eerie
- cute + over-significant
- pathetic + fake sacred

### Add cheapness
- rude line
- less polish
- more synthetic weirdness

### Add symbolic overpressure
- halo
- ring
- contour field
- little fake sigils
- stars/hearts with meaning problems

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Mascot-like imagery becomes much stronger when it carries **cartoonized LSD-hallucination
logic**:
- the mascot implies more than one identity
- the face has recursive or unstable pressure
- the cute symbol feels weirdly over-significant
- the creature could become a sticker, glyph, body part, or warning sign

That keeps it from becoming dead merch sludge.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful anti-failure phrases:
- avoid clean mascot coherence
- avoid corporate-friendly cute design
- keep the creature slightly pathetic and symbolically unstable
- make the mascot contaminated by body glyph logic
- preserve awkwardness and fake significance

## FINAL RULE

A good Garbage Enlightenment mascot should feel like:
