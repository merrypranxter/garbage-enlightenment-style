# Garbage Enlightenment
## Sweet Corruption Acid Cartoon Surrealism
**Garbage Enlightenment** is a style repo for a very specific kind of psychedelic cartoon visual
language: flat, rude, synthetic, bodily, funny, symbolic, low-fi, and constantly on the verge of
mutating into something else.
This style lives in the overlap between:
- acid cartoon surrealism
- bodily glyph symbolism
- mock occult / parody esoterica
- cute corruption
- low-fi digital grotesquerie
- cheap hallucination abstraction
- fast metamorphic loop animation

It is not just “trippy art.”
It is not just “gross cartoon.”
It is not just “cute weird.”
It is not just “occult symbolism.”
It is the unstable family created when all of those are forced into the same cheap glowing
bathtub and told to become one thing.
## Core thesis
This style treats:
- **body parts as symbols**
- **symbols as cartoon matter**
- **cartoon matter as unstable**
- **instability as a feature**
- **intermediate states as meaningful**
- **cheapness as aesthetic truth**
- **cuteness and grotesquerie as compatible**
- **psychedelia as metamorphosis, not ornament**
A hand can be a glyph.
An eye can be a portal.
A mouth can be a joke, a wound, a sticker, or an emblem.
A blob can contain a creature.
A creature can dissolve into a contour field.
A rainbow can be cute, threatening, stupid, sacred-looking, fake-sacred, or all of those at once.

This repo is meant to define, preserve, and operationalize the style for:

## Two linked engines
This style contains two major subsystems:
### 1. Figurative engine
Used for:
- creatures

- faces
- body glyphs
- eyes / mouths / hands / skulls
- fake occult iconography
- cute-corrupted symbolic cartoons
### 2. Abstract engine
Used for:
- contour melt fields
- rainbow topologies
- warped psychedelic backgrounds
- plasma-like distortions
- liquid hallucination textures
- shader-style psychedelic motion spaces
These engines are not separate styles.
They are two metabolic states of the same organism.
## Motion is not optional
Animation is a core part of the style language.
This style often moves through:
- rapid symbolic substitution
- fast contour reassignment
- shape inheritance
- soft identity sliding
- figure-to-pattern leakage
- looped transformation
- cheap dream cadence
- meaningful in-between states
The style is not merely “drawn weird.”
It is often **animated by metamorphosis**.
