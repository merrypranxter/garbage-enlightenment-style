# PALETTE DRIFT NOTES

This file focuses specifically on color drift in Garbage Enlightenment.

Color is one of the fastest ways this style can either:
- snap into identity
or
- fall directly on its ass

Because the style is psychedelic-adjacent, cute-adjacent, grotesque-adjacent, and
occult-adjacent, palette mistakes can easily push it into the wrong neighboring style.

## Core principle

A Garbage Enlightenment palette should feel:

- synthetic
- rude
- emotionally overcommitted
- body-aware
- cheap in a useful way
- symbolically pressurized
- capable of sweetness and contamination at once

When color loses those traits, drift happens fast.

## MAIN PALETTE DRIFT TYPES

---

## 1. TOO PRETTY

### Symptoms
- smooth pleasing harmony
- tasteful surreal color balance
- elegant chroma relationships
- dreamlike beauty without friction

### Why it fails
The style should usually contain some:

- rude clash
- body-tone interruption
- fake color logic
- emotional overstatement

### Fix
Add:
- cavity black
- weird peach flesh
- toxic green interruptions
- hotter accents
- less tasteful transitions

---

## 2. TOO PASTEL / TOO PURE CUTE

### Symptoms
- clean baby-cute palette
- no body tones
- no toxicity
- no chemical edge
- no visual pressure

### Why it fails
Sweetness alone turns the style into safe sticker art.

### Fix
Contaminate with:
- body peach
- mouth red
- bruise mauve
- sour yellow
- black outline emphasis

---

## 3. TOO DARK / TOO GOTHIC

### Symptoms
- black-heavy palette
- desaturated shadow dominance
- generic spooky color logic
- moody elegance
- horror-brand darkness

### Why it fails
Garbage Enlightenment wants symbolic charge, not just gloom.

### Fix
Reintroduce:
- acid yellow
- toy-bright accents
- rude pinks
- synthetic cyan
- sweet signals

---

## 4. TOO GENERIC RAINBOW

### Symptoms
- every hue equally active
- rainbow mush
- no body interruption
- no hierarchy
- default trippy wallpaper

### Why it fails
Rainbow needs pressure and role, not just presence.

### Fix

Use:
- contour-band zoning
- localized highlights
- body-color anchors
- dirty or rude support tones
- better symbolic hierarchy

---

## 5. TOO NATURALISTIC

### Symptoms
- skin tones too realistic
- light behaving realistically
- atmospheric natural color
- creature colors feel biologically plausible in a boring way

### Why it fails
The style benefits from fake color logic.

### Fix
Prompt for:
- synthetic cartoon color
- chemically fake tone

- unnatural pressure zones
- symbolic chroma

---

## 6. TOO CLEANLY DIGITAL

### Symptoms
- over-saturated but polished
- app-store brightness
- too crisp
- too “designed”
- sleek color with no dirt or body

### Why it fails
Digital brightness without cheapness becomes generic design candy.

### Fix
Add:
- dirt candy logic
- flesh interruptions
- compression-like roughness
- rude accent choices

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Palette becomes more Garbage Enlightenment-specific when it reflects **cartoonized
LSD-hallucination logic** through:
- emotionally charged hue boundaries
- one color region feeling too meaningful
- rainbow behavior acting like symbolic transformation instead of decoration
- pressure between sweetness and body color
- fake revelation driven by chroma rather than realism

That is much stronger than just “bright trippy colors.”

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- avoid pretty color harmony
- synthetic acid/body palette tension
- rude candy-toxic color logic
- sweet tones contaminated by bodily chroma
- over-significant contour-band color pressure
- emotionally unstable synthetic palette

## FINAL RULE

When the palette drifts, ask:

**did the color become too tasteful, too pure, too dark, or too generic?**

Then make it a little more chemically stupid.
