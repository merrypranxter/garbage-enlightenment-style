# SOFT GROSSNESS NOTES

This file focuses on **Soft Grossness**, an important tonal/material mode in Garbage
Enlightenment.

Soft Grossness is where the image includes:
- mouths
- teeth
- tongues
- body blobs
- cavities
- drips
- weird anatomy

but in a way that remains:
- cartooned
- affectionate
- playful
- low-fi
- synthetic
- non-cruel
- not fully horror

## Core principle

Soft Grossness is not:
- realistic gore
- flesh horror
- cruelty spectacle
- hard body-horror
- severe disgust

It is:
- body humor
- soft disgustingness
- affectionate wrongness
- sticky symbolic weirdness
- cartoon body intensity

- grossness with tenderness or play still alive

## WHY SOFT GROSSNESS MATTERS

Without it, the style can become:
- too cute
- too clean
- too decorative
- too safe
- too spiritually airy

Soft Grossness anchors the repo in:
- body
- mess
- sensation
- humor
- low-status life
- vulnerable materiality

## MAIN SOFT GROSSNESS SOURCES

### Mouths
Especially black-cavity mouths or too-intense lip seams.

### Teeth
Especially when they are:
- a little too many
- weirdly cute
- soft threatening
- emblematic

### Tongues
Great for:
- body humor
- childish grossness
- softness with weird body charge

### Blobs
Ideal for:
- proto-body weirdness
- soft flesh logic
- body-symbol crossover

### Cavity seams
Useful for making cute shapes behave bodily.

## GOOD SOFT GROSSNESS QUALITIES

- non-realistic
- simplified
- slightly funny
- slightly tender
- visually blunt
- symbolically useful
- soft rather than brutal

## BAD SOFT GROSSNESS

### Too hard
Becomes horror realism.

### Too childish-empty
Becomes crude joke art with no depth.

### Too random
Becomes meaningless goo.

### Too slick
Becomes fetishized body design.

## SOFT GROSSNESS + LSD-LOGIC

Soft Grossness becomes especially on-brand when it reflects **cartoonized LSD-hallucination
logic**:
- body and symbol fuse without warning
- sweetness turns bodily fast
- one contour supports multiple emotional readings
- grossness feels chemically vivid rather than grim

That is a very strong mid-tone region of the style.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- soft grossness
- affectionate body weirdness
- cartoon cavity logic
- sticky symbolic anatomy
- gross but tender
- body humor under fake sacred pressure
- sweetly disgusting in a low-fi way

## FINAL RULE

Soft Grossness in Garbage Enlightenment should feel like:
**a little body problem that somehow stayed emotionally approachable**

# DECAL BEHAVIOR NOTES

This file explains decal behavior in Garbage Enlightenment.

A decal is like a sticker, but with more emphasis on:
- application
- transfer
- layering
- contamination
- surface presence
- portable symbolic identity

Decal behavior is useful for thinking about how symbols move through the style’s visual world.

## Core principle

A decal-like symbol should feel like:
- it can be placed on something
- it can infect another image
- it can be layered into fields, creatures, signs, or icons
- it retains identity while entering new contexts
- it acts like a portable symbolic parasite in a good way

## WHY DECAL BEHAVIOR MATTERS

This style often works through:
- portable symbols
- motif reuse
- sticker-sheet logic
- emblem systems
- symbolic contamination

Thinking in decal terms helps keep symbols:
- reusable
- blunt
- modular
- graphically potent
- mutation-friendly

## MAIN DECAL BEHAVIORS

---

## 1. ATTACHMENT

A decal attaches to:
- a creature

- a face
- a blob
- a field
- a sign
- a poster
- a halo frame

Effect:
- contamination
- symbolic upgrade
- layered identity

---

## 2. INFECTION

A decal changes the host it lands on.

Examples:
- star decal turns into watcher logic
- mouth decal gives a heart body weirdness
- halo decal makes a blob fake sacred
- eye decal gives a symbol consciousness pressure

This is extremely on-brand.

---

## 3. LAYERING

Multiple decal units stack.

Examples:
- eye over heart
- halo over hand
- stars around skull
- sigils over field
- mini glyphs around a badge

This helps create:
- symbolic density
- ritual bureaucracy
- sticker theology
- over-significance

---

## 4. REPEATABILITY

A decal can appear:
- once as focal icon
- many times as swarm
- at multiple scales
- in different tonal branches

This is crucial for building systems.

## GOOD DECAL UNITS

- eye icons
- mouth emblems
- hand symbols
- heart-mouth hybrids
- star-watchers
- fake sigils
- skull badges
- little creature heads
- contour field badges

## BAD DECAL BEHAVIOR

### Too integrated

Loses portability and becomes too scene-bound.

### Too clean
Feels like merch/product branding.

### Too empty
Does not alter the host image.

### Too random
Layering feels chaotic rather than systemic.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Decal behavior becomes especially Garbage Enlightenment-specific when:
- a symbol remains itself while changing the thing it touches
- meaning transfers by attachment
- simple icons create over-significance through layering
- one decal carries several roles at once

That is a very useful modular form of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:

- decal-like symbolic layer
- portable contamination icon
- reusable body glyph decal
- sticker parasite motif
- attachable fake sacred emblem
- layered symbolic decal logic

## FINAL RULE

A decal in Garbage Enlightenment should feel like:
**a portable little symbol that can latch onto anything and make it weirder, more meaningful, and
less trustworthy**
