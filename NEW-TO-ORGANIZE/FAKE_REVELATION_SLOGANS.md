This file contains short slogan-like phrases, mottoes, and fake-serious symbolic captions for the
Mock Revelation side of Garbage Enlightenment.

These are useful for:
- poster ideas
- image concepts
- fake cult merch
- sticker sheets
- title cards
- internal vibes
- tonal calibration

## Core principle

A good fake revelation slogan should feel:
- over-meaningful
- low-budget sacred
- slightly stupid
- body-haunted
- weirdly sincere
- not fully trustworthy
- maybe a little funny without becoming pure joke
