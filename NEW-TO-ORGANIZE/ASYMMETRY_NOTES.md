# ASYMMETRY NOTES

This file explains the role of asymmetry in Garbage Enlightenment.

Asymmetry is one of the style’s most important structural tools because it helps keep the image:
- unstable

- alive
- mutational
- rude
- anti-polish
- emotionally weird

## Core principle

Perfect symmetry often pushes the style toward:
- solemnity
- stiffness
- sacred over-authority
- polished logo energy
- generic psychedelic balance
- dead compositional control

Garbage Enlightenment usually benefits from:

- slight asymmetry
- uneven pressure
- lopsided face logic
- one side more wrong than the other
- imbalance that still feels intentional

## WHERE ASYMMETRY HELPS MOST

---

## 1. FACES

Asymmetry in faces creates:
- mutational tension
- emotional weirdness
- instability of identity
- anti-portrait energy

Examples:
- one eye larger
- mouth off-center
- one cheek becoming contour field
- one side more creature-like

---

## 2. ICONS

A slightly asymmetrical icon often feels:
- less corporate
- less sacred in the wrong way
- more alive
- more body-contaminated

Examples:
- halo ring slightly broken
- heart not perfectly mirrored
- eye shape a little wrong
- hand fingers unevenly tense

---

## 3. CREATURES

Asymmetry prevents the creature from becoming:
- mascot-clean
- concept-art polished
- too coherent

Examples:
- one leg shorter

- one eye heavier
- body weight tilted
- contour bulge on one side

---

## 4. ABSTRACT FIELDS

Even abstract systems benefit from:
- uneven pressure zones
- off-center emergence
- imperfect repetition
- unstable contour weights

This helps the field feel:
- body-haunted
- not purely decorative

- more likely to produce meaning

## LEVELS OF ASYMMETRY

### Mild asymmetry
Best for:
- icons
- stickers
- cute modes
- print graphics

### Moderate asymmetry
Best for:
- creatures
- face mutations
- revelation posters
- field-figure hybrids

### Strong asymmetry
Best for:
- full freak outputs
- body-heavy work
- unstable abstract systems
- motion-driven prompts

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Asymmetry strongly supports cartoonized LSD-hallucination structure because it helps the
image feel:
- unstable
- over-read
- semantically shifting
- slightly unable to hold itself in one clean form

Perfect symmetry often kills this by making the image too final and too resolved.

## WHAT TO AVOID

- accidental sloppiness with no purpose
- imbalance so extreme readability collapses
- symmetry used so strongly it becomes visionary-art grandeur

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- slight asymmetrical pressure
- one side more mutated than the other
- off-center symbolic tension
- lopsided icon logic
- imperfectly sacred
- asymmetry preserving aliveness
