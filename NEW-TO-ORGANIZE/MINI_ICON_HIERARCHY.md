# MINI ICON HIERARCHY

This file explains hierarchy among small icons, decorative motifs, and support symbols in
Garbage Enlightenment.

This matters because the style often uses:
- little stars
- mini eyes
- tiny mouths
- fake sigils
- bubbles
- halos
- creature heads
- small emblems

If all of them are equal, the image can become:
- muddy
- cluttered
- dead wallpaper
- random noise

## Core principle

Mini icons should feel like:
- part of a family
- hierarchically staged
- emotionally supportive
- symbolically pressurized
- not all shouting the same volume at once

## MAIN MINI ICON ROLES

---

## 1. PRIMARY SUPPORT ICON

### Description
The most important small icon besides the main subject.

### Job
- echo the main symbol
- contaminate it
- support tone
- strengthen hierarchy

### Example
A big eye icon with one smaller heart-eye supporting it.

---

## 2. SECONDARY CLUTTER ICON

### Description
Smaller motifs that support the atmosphere.

### Job
- build swarm logic
- create decorative pressure
- expand the visual system

### Example
Little stars, mini sigils, small mouth marks.

---

## 3. RHYTHM ICON

### Description

Repeated mini units used mainly for visual pacing.

### Job
- create recurrence
- establish symbolic chant
- animate the space

### Example
Three small eyes, several stars, tiny bubbles.

---

## 4. TONE ICON

### Description
A mini icon whose job is mostly tonal.

### Job
- sweeten
- grossify
- add menace
- soften sacredness
- add decorative sadness

### Example
One tiny little sad star.
One little tooth.
One little halo blob.

## GOOD MINI ICON HIERARCHY RULES

### Rule 1
One main symbol still dominates.

### Rule 2
Only one or two mini-icon families should be strongly emphasized.

### Rule 3
Mini icons should echo, contaminate, or support the larger logic.

### Rule 4
Variation in size and spacing matters.

### Rule 5
At least some mini icons should feel slightly alive or role-unstable.

## BAD MINI ICON HIERARCHY

### Flat clutter
Everything same importance.

### Too decorative
Mini icons become meaningless sparkles.

### Too many motif families
No coherence.

### Too much neatness
Turns into design layout instead of symbolic ecosystem.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Mini icon hierarchy becomes especially on-brand when:
- little motifs feel over-significant
- small support icons seem to share consciousness with the main symbol
- repetition creates a sense of symbolic crowding or emergence
- mini forms feel like smaller versions of the same unstable meaning event

That’s a very useful static rendering of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- hierarchically staged mini icons
- symbolic support clutter with pressure
- repeated mini motifs that echo the main glyph
- small icons acting like a chorus around the central sign
- over-significant support symbols

## FINAL RULE

Mini icons in Garbage Enlightenment should feel like:
**little assistants helping the main symbol become more emotionally weird and more convinced
of its own importance**

# WARNING BADGE RECIPES

This file contains recipes for making warning-badge imagery in Garbage Enlightenment.

Warning badges are useful because they combine:
- symbol clarity
- sticker portability
- fake authority
- body glyph logic
- ritual bureaucracy
- Soft Menace
- Cheap Holiness

A warning badge is stronger than a generic symbol because it feels like:
- it has a job
- it belongs to a system
- it is trying to inform, caution, or bless
- it may not fully understand its own instructions

## Core principle

A Garbage Enlightenment warning badge should feel like:
- a little official

- a little wrong
- body-made
- emotionally overcommitted
- visually blunt
- able to work on a sticker sheet, poster, patch, or emblem board

It should not feel like:
- modern UI safety signage
- polished corporate iconography
- luxury occult branding
- generic spooky merch

## RECIPE 1 — BODY WARNING BADGE

### Formula
Choose:
- eye
- mouth
- hand
- skull
- heart-mouth hybrid
- blob glyph

Put it inside:

- circle
- triangle
- soft badge shape
- seal outline
- halo frame

Then add:
- one contaminant
- one support clutter family
- one anti-polish cue

### Example
A circular warning badge with an eye-mouth hybrid in the center, rude black outline, acid yellow
and weird peach palette, little stars around the edge, and a broken halo ring to fake authority
while keeping it weird

---

## RECIPE 2 — SWEET WARNING BADGE

### Formula
Choose:
- star
- heart
- rainbow

- dolphin
- cloud

Then contaminate with:
- eye
- teeth
- cavity
- fake sacred framing
- body seam

### Example
A triangle badge with a star-watcher icon, little teeth hidden in one point, rude black outline,
sweet toxin palette, and symbolic over-meaning that makes the cute sign feel slightly watchful

---

## RECIPE 3 — REVELATION WARNING BADGE

### Formula
Choose:
- eye pyramid
- halo hand
- portal seal
- sigil face
- fake sacred heart

Then emphasize:
- centered icon force
- badge portability
- cheap symbolic seriousness
- ritual bureaucracy tone

### Example
A soft-edged warning badge containing a haloed hand with a palm-eye, contour-band aura, little
fake sigils, and a mood of low-budget holy caution

---

## RECIPE 4 — CREATURE WARNING BADGE

### Formula
Take a little creature and compress it into:
- one badge silhouette
- one face event
- one symbolic support system
- one emotional contradiction

### Example
A little blob goblin inside a faux-official badge, big overcommitted eyes, tiny feet, halo ring, and a
look like it is both mascot and caution notice

## WARNING BADGE SUPPORT TOOLS

Useful supports:
- broken rings
- stars
- mini eyes
- little fake sigils
- contour bands
- soft clouds
- tiny mouths
- badge scallops if kept rude, not elegant

## TONE TARGETS

Good badge tones:
- fake caution
- sweet menace
- body-sign authority
- ritual bureaucracy
- low-budget sacredness
- decorative dread
- chemically vivid warning energy

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Warning badges become more Garbage Enlightenment-specific when:
- a simple sign feels too emotionally loaded
- a cute symbol becomes a caution glyph
- the badge implies multiple symbolic jobs at once
- the warning feels both ridiculous and weirdly valid

That is a compact form of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic.

## FINAL RULE

A Garbage Enlightenment warning badge should feel like:
**a small official sign from a spiritual sticker department that has already made several
procedural mistakes**
