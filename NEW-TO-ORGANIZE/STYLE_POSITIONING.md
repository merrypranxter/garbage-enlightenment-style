# STYLE POSITIONING
This document situates Garbage Enlightenment relative to neighboring styles, reference zones,
and aesthetic families.
The goal is not to flatten it into one pre-existing category.
The goal is to explain what it is adjacent to, what it borrows from, and what makes it its own
mutant thing.
## Core positioning statement
Garbage Enlightenment is a style of **low-fi psychedelic cartoon surrealism** built from:
- bodily glyph logic
- mock esoterica
- sweet corruption
- mutant creaturehood
- cheap hallucination fields
- rude graphic simplification
- rapid metamorphic logic
It sits near several known aesthetic territories, but does not fully collapse into any of them.
## Closest neighboring zones
### 1. Psychedelic cartoon surrealism
This is one of the closest broad umbrellas.
Shared traits:
- altered-state logic
- symbolic instability
- visual metamorphosis
- anti-naturalistic imagery
- synthetic intensity
Difference:
Garbage Enlightenment is more:
- rude

- bodily
- cheap
- sticker-like
- fake-sacred
- prankster
- cute-contaminated
It is less:
- ornate
- transcendent
- majestic
- reverent
--### 2. Outsider / low-fi underground cartooning
This is another close neighbor.
Shared traits:
- anti-polish
- body weirdness
- awkwardness
- direct line
- emotional wrongness
- low-prestige visual intelligence
Difference:
Garbage Enlightenment is usually more:
- psychedelic
- symbolic
- color-charged
- abstract-field aware
- occult-parody inflected
- loop/morph oriented
--### 3. Grotesque pop / lowbrow mutation
There is overlap with pop grotesquerie and mutant cartoon imagery.
Shared traits:
- body fragments
- comic horror
- weird creatures

- symbolic exaggeration
Difference:
Garbage Enlightenment is less:
- rendered
- polished
- collectible-designer-toy slick
And more:
- cheap
- graphic
- hallucinatory
- field-based
- LSD-logic adjacent
- emotionally unstable
--### 4. Mock occult / conspiratorial iconography
This is a strong symbolic neighbor.
Shared traits:
- eyes
- pyramids
- sigils
- aura rings
- fake revelation
- symbolic overcharge
Difference:
Garbage Enlightenment does not usually commit to:
- solemnity
- crisp sacred branding
- serious ritual orthodoxy
- austere esoteric elegance
It keeps the symbol bodily, low-fi, and slightly ridiculous.
--### 5. Cute corruption / anti-pristine sweetness
This is a very important neighbor cluster.
Shared traits:

- stars

- rainbow sweetness
- decorative toy-like motifs
- emotionally approachable imagery
Difference:
Garbage Enlightenment resists:
- pure kawaii
- wholesome polish

- innocent fantasy sweetness

- mutation
- body symbols

--### 6. Abstract psychedelic shader aesthetics
This is crucial for the non-figurative subsystem.
Shared traits:
- contour fields
- warped bands
- thermal melts

- flow-based emergence
Difference:
Garbage Enlightenment uses abstraction as:
- mutation substrate
- symbolic pressure field
- body-haunted visual soup
- figure-birth space
Not just decorative trippy wallpaper.
## More distant but relevant neighbors
These are reference-adjacent, but not core identity.

### Visionary art
Relevant only in the sense of:
- altered-state ambition
- symbolic weight
- cosmic implication
But Garbage Enlightenment rejects:
- noble transcendence
- polished spiritual revelation
- luminous sincerity played straight
--### 1960s psychedelic poster traditions
Relevant only in the broad lineage of psychedelic visual distortion and graphic stylization.
But Garbage Enlightenment is usually:
- less ornate
- less typographic
- less filigreed
- less poster-elegant
- more bodily
- more cartoon mutant
--### Kawaii / cute sticker visual languages
Relevant because of sweet decorative motifs.
But Garbage Enlightenment remains:
- rougher
- weirder
- more contaminated
- more body-symbolic
- less pure and less polished
--### Glitch / digital damage aesthetics
Relevant because of:
- cheapness
- signal instability
- low-fi digital feel

But the repo is not about generic glitch style.
It only uses digital damage when it supports:
- mutation
- symbolic emergence
- synthetic cartoon wrongness
## LSD-hallucination relation
One of the style’s clearest positioning anchors is that it often resembles **cartoonized
LSD-hallucination logic**.
This means it often shares:
- rapid significance shifts
- over-meaning
- symbolic compression
- forms becoming other forms
- unstable figure-ground boundaries
- recursive implication
- pattern/being crossover
- bodily and cosmic scales colliding
But this resemblance is not:
- literal medical depiction
- strict perceptual simulation
- polished visionary documentation
It is better understood as:
**hallucinatory structure translated into rude symbolic cartoon language**
That distinction is important.
## Unique combination
What makes Garbage Enlightenment distinct is not any one ingredient by itself.
It is the coexistence of all of these at once:
- rude graphic simplification
- body glyphs
- sweet contamination
- parody esoterica
- mutant creatures
- abstract hallucination fields
- low-fi digital cheapness

- fake revelation tone
- fast morph logic
- cartoonized LSD-structure
Most neighboring styles only carry some of these.
## Final positioning sentence
Garbage Enlightenment belongs in the weird corridor between:
**psychedelic cartoon surrealism, low-fi body-symbol grotesque, mock occult sticker logic, sweet
corruption, and shader-haunted hallucination design**
That is its home turf.
