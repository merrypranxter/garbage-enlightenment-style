# EYE ICON NOTES

This file focuses specifically on the eye as an icon in Garbage Enlightenment.

The eye is one of the style’s most powerful and reusable symbols.
It is a perfect carrier for:
- watchfulness
- fake revelation
- paranoia
- symbolic pressure
- portal logic
- decorative repetition
- body-haunted emotional charge

## Core principle

An eye icon should not merely depict an eye.

It should feel like:
- a watcher
- a seal
- a warning
- a fake holy sign
- a portal
- a sticker
- a consciousness node
- a body-sign pretending to be a logo

## WHY THE EYE IS SO STRONG

The eye can easily slide between roles:
- body part
- symbol
- surveillance icon
- ritual emblem
- portal opening
- decorative motif
- recursive pattern node

That makes it one of the best vehicles for the style’s mutational symbolism.

## MAIN EYE ICON MODES

---

## 1. SINGLE WATCHER EYE

### Description
One large eye carrying most of the symbolic charge.

### Effect
- immediate focus
- fake sacred authority
- strong icon poster potential
- simple but intense

### Best pairings
- rings
- triangles
- stars
- contour bands
- mouths

---

## 2. EYE IN SYMBOL

### Description
The eye is placed inside:
- pyramid
- heart
- star
- hand
- blob
- rainbow
- portal

### Effect
- instant contamination
- increased symbolic instability
- role crossover
- strong Sweet Corruption or Mock Revelation use

---

## 3. REPEATED EYE FIELD

### Description
Many small eye icons repeat in a field or swarm.

### Effect
- surveillance pressure
- pattern consciousness
- fake ritual repetition
- cartoonized over-significance

### Best pairings
- stars
- little sigils
- contour bands
- creature swarms

---

## 4. PORTAL EYE

### Description
The eye acts like:
- tunnel
- opening
- threshold
- cavity
- depth event

### Effect
- stronger LSD-logic pressure
- body ↔ cosmos crossover
- fake revelation escalation

## SHAPE NOTES

Useful features:
- simplified almond or oval shape
- strong pupil/iris contrast
- thick symbolic outline
- exaggerated size
- asymmetrical tension when useful
- lashes if they serve symbolic role

Avoid:
- naturalistic realism
- polished beauty eye painting
- over-detailed anatomical eyelid rendering
- fashion-illustration eye glamour

## EYE ICON + LSD-LOGIC

The eye icon becomes especially on-brand when:
- it feels too meaningful
- it acts as body part and cosmic sign at once
- it appears in pattern form and becomes distributed consciousness
- it looks simple but semantically unstable
- it could become a portal, a creature seed, or a warning sticker

That is a near-perfect example of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic in icon form.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- over-significant eye emblem
- fake sacred watcher icon
- portal-eye sticker
- body glyph eye used as warning sign
- repeated eye swarm with symbolic pressure
- cartoon eye behaving like a revelation seal

## FINAL RULE

An eye icon in Garbage Enlightenment should feel like:
**a very simple symbol that is taking itself, and you, a little too seriously**

# HEART CORRUPTION NOTES

This file focuses specifically on the heart as a corrupted sweet symbol in Garbage
Enlightenment.

The heart is one of the most useful Sweet Corruption motifs because it starts with:
- sweetness
- affection
- decoration
- toy logic
- visual simplicity

and can be pushed toward:
- body weirdness
- fake revelation
- mouth logic
- watchfulness
- threat
- symbolic wrongness

## Core principle

A Garbage Enlightenment heart should rarely remain a pure cute heart.

It should often feel like:
- a sweet sign with a body problem
- affection contaminated by weirdness
- a symbol becoming anatomy
- a sticker that learned too much
- a decorative unit under symbolic pressure

## MAIN HEART CORRUPTION MODES

---

## 1. HEART + MOUTH

### Description
The heart opens like a mouth or contains a mouth seam/cavity.

### Effect
- immediate Sweet Corruption
- body ↔ cute fusion
- fake intimacy
- gross-funny charge

---

## 2. HEART + EYE

### Description
The heart becomes watchful.

### Effect
- affection becomes surveillance
- sweetness becomes over-significant
- decorative symbol gains fake revelation pressure

---

## 3. HEART + TEETH

### Description
The heart grows teeth or a bite structure.

### Effect
- soft menace
- sweet danger
- toy body horror without realism

---

## 4. HEART + HALO / RING

### Description
The heart is framed as:
- sacred emblem
- portal sign
- revelation sticker

### Effect
- fake holiness
- over-meaning
- decorative transcendence
- absurd sincerity

---

## 5. HEART + BLOB / BODY MATTER

### Description
The heart softens toward:
- flesh blob
- cavity sign
- drippy icon

- body-adjacent symbol

### Effect
- tenderness becomes bodily
- sweetness loses purity
- more pathos, more weirdness

## GOOD HEART QUALITIES

- simple silhouette
- clear decorative identity
- strong outline
- room for contamination
- emotionally immediate

## WHAT TO AVOID

### Too Valentine's
- commercial holiday sweetness
- romance-first cliché
- decorative charm with no weirdness

### Too grim
- realistic bleeding hearts

- severe body horror
- heavy gore symbolism

### Too polished
- glossy sticker perfection
- luxury cute merch look

## HEART + LSD-LOGIC

The heart becomes especially Garbage Enlightenment-specific when:
- it feels too meaningful for its own simplicity
- it can be read as affection and body opening at once
- sweetness and watchfulness coexist in the same emblem
- the image keeps shifting between decorative and bodily interpretation

That is very strong cartoonized LSD-hallucination structure.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- corrupted heart icon
- sweet symbol with body weirdness
- heart acting like a mouth
- over-significant affectionate emblem

- fake sacred heart sticker
- cute symbol contaminated by anatomy

## FINAL RULE

A Garbage Enlightenment heart should feel like:
**love as a sticker, a mouth, a warning, and a mild hallucination all at once**
