# WARNING SIGN NOTES

This file focuses on warning-sign logic in Garbage Enlightenment.

Warning signs are important because the style often thrives on:
- symbolic clarity
- over-significance
- fake urgency
- body/symbol contamination
- little systems of cartoon authority

A warning sign in this style is usually not literal safety signage.
It is more like:
- a fake cosmic caution

- a body-made omen
- a cheap sacred warning
- a low-budget sign from the wrong metaphysical department

## Core principle

A warning sign should feel like:
- it is trying to warn you
- it might not know exactly what about
- it is emotionally overcommitted
- it is a little ridiculous
- it still has visual authority
- body weirdness has infected the signage system

## WHY WARNING SIGNS WORK

They combine:

- readability
- graphic force
- symbolic pressure
- fake bureaucracy
- cosmic joke energy
- sticker portability

This makes them perfect for:
- posters
- emblems
- icons
- sheets
- fake revelation work

## WARNING SIGN INGREDIENTS

### Basic shapes

- triangles
- circles
- badges
- seals
- rounded warning forms
- frames with halos

### Symbol payload
- eyes
- mouths
- hands
- skulls
- hearts
- stars
- fake sigils
- little creatures

### Contamination
- asymmetry
- body weirdness
- sweet motifs
- fake sacred charge
- mutational pressure

## WARNING SIGN MODES

---

## 1. COSMIC WARNING SIGN

### Description
Feels like a universal warning, but cheap and cartooned.

### Best motifs

- eye
- halo
- portal
- stars
- contour pressure

---

## 2. BODY WARNING SIGN

### Description
A warning sign built from anatomy.

### Best motifs
- mouth
- hand
- skull

- eye
- flesh blob

---

## 3. SWEET WARNING SIGN

### Description
A sign using cute motifs:
- stars
- hearts
- rainbows
- dolphins

but made watchful or contaminated.

### Effect

- perfect Soft Menace
- Sweet Corruption + authority

---

## 4. REVELATION WARNING SIGN

### Description
A sign that behaves like fake holy instruction.

### Effect
- mock esoterica
- over-significance
- cosmic sermon sticker logic

## WHAT TO AVOID

### Too literal
Actual industrial safety sign imitation with no symbolic weirdness.

### Too clean
Corporate vector signage energy.

### Too solemn
High-concept occult decree with no cheapness or body contamination.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Warning signs become more Garbage Enlightenment-specific when they feel:
- too meaningful
- simple but emotionally overcharged
- semantically unstable between warning, blessing, joke, and omen
- like they are telling you something that may not survive translation

That is strong cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic in a graphic design shell.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- fake cosmic warning sign
- body-made caution emblem
- over-significant symbolic notice
- cheap sacred caution sticker
- low-budget revelation sign
- Sweet Corruption warning icon

## FINAL RULE

A Garbage Enlightenment warning sign should feel like:
**a safety notice written by a hallucinating sticker theology department**

# REVELATION POSTER NOTES

This file focuses specifically on revelation-poster logic in Garbage Enlightenment.

A revelation poster is an image that feels like:
- an announcement
- a sermon
- a symbolic warning
- a fake sacred advertisement
- a cheap holy poster
- a cosmic notice
- a body-sign proclamation

It is one of the strongest compositional formats in the repo.

## Core principle

A revelation poster should feel:
- over-significant
- graphic
- body-contaminated
- weirdly official
- low-budget holy
- rude
- poster-readable
- emotionally unstable

It should not feel like:
- polished spiritual poster art
- mystical luxury decor
- solemn ceremonial imagery
- tasteful visionary advertising

## CORE REVELATION POSTER STRUCTURE

A strong revelation poster often includes:

### 1. One dominant icon
- eye
- hand
- face
- skull
- heart-mouth hybrid
- portal symbol
- body goblin emblem

### 2. Fake sacred support
- halo
- rings
- contour aura

- symbolic framing
- little sigils

### 3. Secondary contamination
- stars
- hearts
- extra eyes
- mouths
- little symbols
- body weirdness

### 4. Flat symbolic stage
- void background
- contour-band field
- decorative clutter
- minimal environment

## REVELATION POSTER MODES

---

## 1. EYE REVELATION POSTER

### Description
An eye dominates the frame and feels:
- watchful
- too important
- spiritually overcommitted
- slightly ridiculous

### Good supports
- pyramid
- halo
- stars

- contour bands

---

## 2. HAND REVELATION POSTER

### Description
A hand becomes:
- saint icon
- portal sign
- warning sticker
- body prophecy emblem

### Good supports
- aura ring
- palm eye
- stars

- fake sigils

---

## 3. HEART REVELATION POSTER

### Description
A heart is elevated to fake sacred significance and contaminated into body weirdness.

### Good supports
- halo
- eyes
- mouth seam
- decorative stars

---

## 4. CREATURE REVELATION POSTER

### Description
A little being is staged like:
- saint
- omen
- relic
- cosmic announcement

### Effect
- excellent for freak tenderness + fake revelation overlap

## TEXTURE / SURFACE NOTES

A revelation poster often benefits from:
- low-fi print feeling
- slight cheapness

- rude color
- anti-polish flatness
- sticker/emblem readability

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Revelation posters become especially Garbage Enlightenment-specific when:
- the image feels too meaningful for what it is
- simple symbols behave like they are transmitting more than they should
- body and sacred roles contaminate each other
- poster clarity hides deeper symbolic instability

This is one of the clearest static forms of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:

- fake revelation poster
- low-budget sacred announcement
- body-symbol proclamation image
- over-significant sticker sermon
- cheap transcendence poster logic
- cosmic bureaucracy notice in mutant cartoon form

## FINAL RULE

A Garbage Enlightenment revelation poster should feel like:
**a sacred flyer from a spiritual movement that formed yesterday and already has
merchandising problems**
