# EVALUATION CHECKLIST
Use this checklist to evaluate whether an image, animation, prompt, or shader output actually
belongs in Garbage Enlightenment.
This is not a purity test.
It is a diagnostic tool.
## CORE IDENTITY CHECK
### 1. Is there a strong graphic-symbolic subject?
Examples:
- eye
- mouth
- hand
- face
- creature
- pyramid
- contour-field emergence
If no, the image may be too vague.
### 2. Is there at least one body-glyph or body-weirdness anchor?

If no, the style may be drifting into generic psychedelic or abstract territory.
### 3. Does the image feel at least slightly contaminated?
Contamination can mean:
- mutation
- asymmetry
- wrongness
- bodily intrusion
- fake-esoteric charge
- cute corruption
If no, it may be too pure.
### 4. Is the line/shape language sufficiently rude or low-fi?
If no, the style may be over-polished.
### 5. Does the image avoid full solemnity?
If no, it may be drifting toward reverent occult or visionary art.
## TONE CHECK
### 6. Is there tonal friction?
Examples:
- cute + gross
- sweet + ominous
- revelation + nonsense
- decorative + bodily
- pathetic + cosmic
If no, the image may be too one-note.
### 7. Does it feel emotionally unstable in an intentional way?
If no, it may be too safe or too finished.
### 8. Is it more fake-sacred / prankster than truly noble?
If no, check for solemnity drift.
## FORM CHECK
### 9. Are the forms compressed enough to stay graphic and cartoon-legible?
If no, it may be drifting toward polished surreal illustration.
### 10. Does the image imply mutational possibility?
Even a still image should feel like it could become something else.

If no, it may be too static.
### 11. If abstract, does the field feel like it could produce a symbol or creature?
If no, it may be empty abstraction.
## CUTE CORRUPTION CHECK
### 12. If cute motifs are present, are they sufficiently contaminated or destabilized?
If no, it may be drifting into clean kawaii or sticker art.
### 13. If no cute motifs are present, does the image still leave room for playfulness or
absurdity?
If no, it may be too grim.
## OCCULT / SYMBOL CHECK
### 14. If esoteric or occult motifs are present, are they weird, bodily, or absurd enough?
If no, it may be drifting into sleek occult branding or solemn mysticism.
## LSD-LOGIC CHECK
### 15. Does the image resemble cartoonized LSD-hallucination structure in any meaningful
way?
Examples:
- one form implying another
- significance overload
- pattern/figure instability
- recursive implication
- symbolic over-meaning
- continuous transformation logic
If no, then calling it “LSD-adjacent” may be lazy and inaccurate.
## MOTION CHECK (for animation or implied motion)
### 16. Does the motion rely on metamorphosis more than ordinary movement?
If no, it may not be using the style’s strongest motion system.
### 17. Are intermediate states meaningful?
If no, the loop may be too generic.
### 18. Does the image feel like it is discovering itself in real time?
If yes, good.

## FINAL SCORE IDEA
A strong Garbage Enlightenment image usually hits:
- body glyph logic
- rude graphic compression
- contamination
- tonal friction
- symbolic pressure
- mutational potential
You do not need every single trait maxed out.
But if too many are missing, the style collapses into something weaker.
## FINAL QUESTION
Ask:
**Does this feel like a cheap, symbol-rich, bodily, cute-contaminated, fake-sacred cartoon
hallucination trying to become several things at once?**
If yes, you’re probably in the right cursed neighborhood.
