# MOUTH ICON NOTES

This file focuses specifically on the mouth as an icon in Garbage Enlightenment.

The mouth is one of the repo’s strongest symbols because it can carry:
- body weirdness
- humor
- threshold logic
- vulnerability
- grossness
- sweetness
- fake erotic charge
- fake sacred threshold energy

## Core principle

A mouth icon should not just be “a mouth drawing.”

It should feel like:
- a sticker
- a warning
- a cavity
- a portal
- a joke
- a body-sign
- an emotional puncture mark
- a decorative unit that became too meaningful

## WHY THE MOUTH WORKS SO WELL

The mouth is naturally unstable in role.
It can read as:
- anatomy
- symbol
- opening
- wound
- smile
- threat
- soft decorative shape
- shameful little portal

That role instability is perfect for this style.

## MOUTH SHAPE NOTES

Useful shape traits:
- horizontal emphasis
- lip seam
- black interior
- tooth interruptions
- tongue option
- asymmetry
- slightly exaggerated curve

Avoid:
- overly natural lips

- glossy beauty illustration mouth
- realistic shading emphasis
- over-sexy finish that kills the rude weirdness

## MOUTH ICON + LSD-LOGIC

The mouth icon becomes especially strong when it feels:
- like an opening and a symbol at once
- like it could become a portal
- like it might be funny and profound at the same time
- like one simple body part is carrying too much significance

That is excellent cartoonized LSD-hallucination structure.
