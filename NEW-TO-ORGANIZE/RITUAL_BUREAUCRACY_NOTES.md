# RITUAL BUREAUCRACY NOTES

This file focuses on one of the funniest and most useful conceptual tones in Garbage
Enlightenment:

**Ritual Bureaucracy**

This is the feeling that the symbols, icons, warnings, halos, hands, portals, stickers, and
creatures all belong to:
- a fake administration
- a cosmic office
- a low-budget holy department
- a symbolic filing system
- a cult with too much paperwork
- a metaphysical government that prints stickers instead of laws

This tone is powerful because it blends:
- authority
- absurdity
- fake revelation
- graphic clarity
- symbolic over-meaning
- emotional cheapness

## Core principle

Ritual Bureaucracy should feel like:
- an official process exists
- that process is spiritually suspicious
- the forms are symbolic and bodily
- the warnings are weirdly sincere
- the icons have rank for some reason
- nobody fully understands the system, but it keeps issuing decals

## WHAT MAKES IT WORK

### 1. Signage logic
Symbols feel procedural or administrative.

### 2. Badge logic

Icons feel like categories, approvals, warnings, or seals.

### 3. Fake sanctity
The system acts as if it is holy, but something about it is obviously off.

### 4. Body contamination
The bureaucracy keeps accidentally using:
- eyes
- mouths
- hands
- skulls
- hearts
- flesh logic

This makes it feel very on-brand.

## GOOD RITUAL BUREAUCRACY SUBJECTS

- fake warning sign systems
- sheets of body glyph approvals
- revelation posters that feel like notices
- stamped portal emblems
- eye pyramid classifications
- little haloed instruction icons

- swarms of official-looking symbols with no real stable doctrine

## TONE EFFECTS

Ritual Bureaucracy creates:
- goofy cosmic dread
- symbolic comedy
- fake sacred authority
- low-budget transcendence
- emotionally overcommitted signage
- body bureaucrat weirdness

## WHAT TO AVOID

### Too serious
Turns into genuine occult doctrine.

### Too corporate
Turns into modern UI/branding parody instead of symbolic weirdness.

### Too random
Loses the feeling of system or administration.

### Too polished

Stops feeling cheap and alive.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Ritual Bureaucracy becomes especially Garbage Enlightenment-specific when it taps
**cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic**:
- simple symbols feel like official cosmic statements
- significance is assigned too hard and too fast
- decorative marks behave like categories of meaning
- the visual system feels administratively sacred for no stable reason

This is a great way to organize a whole batch of imagery under one tone.
