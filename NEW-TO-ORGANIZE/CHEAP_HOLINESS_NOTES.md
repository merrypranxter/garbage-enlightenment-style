# CHEAP HOLINESS NOTES

This file focuses on one of the most central tonal/material concepts in Garbage Enlightenment:

**Cheap Holiness**

Cheap Holiness is where:
- sacred framing
- halo logic
- revelation pressure
- ritual seriousness
- icon staging

are filtered through:
- low-fi graphics
- body weirdness
- anti-polish energy
- fake significance
- emotional overcommitment
- sticker logic
- underfunded transcendence

## Core principle

Cheap Holiness should feel like:
- a holy image made badly but sincerely
- a sacred badge with low production value
- transcendence under economic pressure

- a revelation image that came out wrong but still insists
- body-made sanctity
- fake sacredness that still carries charge

It should not feel like:
- polished devotional art
- elegant mysticism
- luxury occult branding
- noble transcendence design
- premium spiritual aesthetics

## WHAT CREATES CHEAP HOLINESS

### 1. Sacred framing
- halos
- aura rings
- centered icon composition
- emblem staging
- symbolic symmetry with contamination

### 2. Anti-luxury graphics
- rude outlines
- flat color
- blunt shapes

- print-like cheapness
- sticker clarity

### 3. Bodily contamination
- eyes
- mouths
- hands
- blobs
- skulls
- body pressure inside sacred forms

### 4. Emotional overcommitment
The image tries too hard to mean something.
That effort is part of the holiness.

## CHEAP HOLINESS SUBJECTS

- haloed hand
- eye pyramid
- heart-mouth relic
- tiny saintly blob creature
- revelation warning badge
- star-watcher with fake aura
- body glyph framed like a devotional image

## WHAT TO AVOID

### Too polished
Kills the “cheap” part.

### Too ironic and empty
Kills the “holiness” part.

### Too grim
Shifts toward dark occult art.

### Too corporate
Turns into cleaned-up icon branding.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Cheap Holiness becomes highly Garbage Enlightenment-specific when it behaves like
**cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic**:
- a simple symbol feels wildly important
- significance feels chemically amplified
- sacred charge arrives through cheap forms
- the image is ridiculous and convincing at once

That paradox is one of the style’s strongest pleasures.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- cheap holiness
- low-budget sacred icon
- fake holy sticker logic
- body-contaminated devotional emblem
- revelation image with anti-luxury energy
- rude transcendence

## FINAL RULE

Cheap Holiness in Garbage Enlightenment should feel like:
**a holy image printed by a little freak with no budget and too much conviction**

# STICKER SHEET COMPOSITION NOTES

This file focuses on sticker-sheet style compositions in Garbage Enlightenment.

Sticker sheets are one of the style’s most natural formats because they allow:

- portable symbols
- motif repetition
- branch variety
- Sweet Corruption
- body glyph packs
- fake revelation inventories
- creature arrays
- symbolic swarms

## Core principle

A sticker sheet should not feel like:
- clean merch art
- neat UI icon grid
- premium vinyl sticker product
- kawaii collection page
- random clutter board

It should feel like:
- a family of mutant symbols
- a contaminated icon ecology
- a page of body-haunted decals
- a fake sacred sticker bureaucracy
- a modular but emotionally unstable visual chorus

## MAIN STICKER SHEET MODES

---

## 1. BODY GLYPH SHEET

### Contents
- eyes
- mouths
- hands
- skulls
- faces
- blobs

### Goal
Show the body as a symbolic alphabet.

---

## 2. SWEET CORRUPTION SHEET

### Contents
- stars

- hearts
- rainbows
- dolphins
- cute little faces
- sweet body hybrids

### Goal
Show decorative sweetness under contamination pressure.

---

## 3. MOCK REVELATION SHEET

### Contents
- eye pyramids
- halo hands
- fake seals
- portals
- warning signs
- little sigils

### Goal
Create a fake sacred administration of symbols.

---

## 4. CREATURE SHEET

### Contents
- body goblins
- face-creatures
- pathetic mini beasts
- blob familiars
- cute-corrupted animals

### Goal
Build a little creature family without making them too clean or marketable.

## SHEET COMPOSITION RULES

### Rule 1
Use variety within family resemblance.
All symbols should feel like cousins, not strangers.

### Rule 2
Keep each sticker readable as a portable unit.
Do not let the sheet become a muddy scene.

### Rule 3
Vary size and density slightly.
Perfect grid regularity can kill the life.

### Rule 4
Use contamination strategically.
Not every symbol must be equally intense, but none should be completely dead.

### Rule 5
Let small motifs echo across the sheet.
This creates symbolic system feeling.

## DECORATIVE SUPPORTS

Useful supports:
- little stars
- bubbles
- fake sigils
- contour scraps
- tiny eyes
- sweet clutter
- low-fi aura fragments

These should support the sheet without flattening hierarchy.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

A sticker sheet becomes especially on-brand when:
- multiple simple symbols feel over-significant together
- motifs seem to share symbolic pressure
- some stickers could almost become others
- the page reads like a modular hallucination system rather than product inventory

That is a great static form of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- body-haunted sticker sheet
- modular Sweet Corruption decal page
- fake sacred sticker bureaucracy
- low-fi mutant icon pack
- family of contaminated symbolic decals
- over-significant sticker swarm

## FINAL RULE

A Garbage Enlightenment sticker sheet should feel like:

**a page of little cursed decals all auditioning for religious significance**
