# ABSTRACT HALLUCINATION FIELDS
Garbage Enlightenment includes a major non-figurative subsystem: abstract psychedelic fields
that behave like shader spaces, melted contour maps, liquid signal distortions, false
topographies, and chemical cartoon plasma.
These are not separate from the figurative style.
They are part of the same universe.
## Core principle
Abstract fields in this style should feel like:
- hallucination matter
- visual signal soup
- symbolic pre-forms
- psychedelic texture space
- mutation substrate

- cheap cosmic weather
- digital melt environments
They are especially useful as:
- backgrounds
- portal interiors
- aura zones
- transitional spaces
- figure-birth fields
- shader templates
- motion engines
- pattern-to-figure bridge material
## Main families of abstract fields
--## 1. CONTOUR-BAND FIELDS
These look like:
- wavy topographic bands
- psychedelic isolines
- melted stacked color regions
- shifting boundaries between chromatic zones
### Good qualities
- clear band separation
- synthetic colors
- slow-to-fast warping
- contour edges that feel elastic
- topography without realism
These are excellent for:
- hands
- silhouettes
- emblematic icons
- aura-like staging
- looping distortions
--## 2. OIL-SLICK / THERMAL FIELDS
These look like:

- metallic psychedelic maps
- heat-spectrum distortions
- rainbow pressure gradients
- impossible liquid color logic
### Good qualities
- false iridescence
- hard-to-soft transitions
- chemical color collisions
- unstable contour emphasis
- surface that feels like image and liquid at once
These can push the style toward:
- fake revelation
- synthetic trance
- gross beauty
- portal-space
--## 3. PLASMA BLOBS
These are more amorphous and flow-heavy.
### Good qualities
- cellular swelling
- blob-to-blob transition
- field pressure
- thresholded regions
- breathing graphic masses
Useful for:
- creature substrate
- goo environments
- pre-symbolic body matter
- morph transition backdrops
--## 4. FALSE TOPOGRAPHIES
These fields suggest:
- maps
- terrains

- pressure zones
- folded visual geography
But they do not need to correspond to real space.
They are useful because they imply:
- buried form
- hidden pattern
- latent emergence
- the sense that something is waiting to appear
This is particularly useful when a figure emerges from the field later.
--## 5. SIGNAL DAMAGE / CHEAP DIGITAL HALLUCINATION
These can include:
- dither
- scanline-ish interference
- pixel swim
- low-fi blur
- compression-like instability
- feedback dirt
- threshold noise
- digital shimmer
These effects should support the image’s synthetic weirdness without turning it into generic
glitch design.
## Main jobs of abstract fields
### 1. Atmosphere
They create psychedelic space without requiring fully rendered environments.
### 2. Transformation substrate
They give figures something to emerge from or dissolve into.
### 3. Symbolic extension
They make body glyphs or esoteric icons feel cosmically embedded.
### 4. Motion logic
They can do the moving when characters do not.

### 5. Cheap transcendence
They can make an image feel visionary, but in the repo’s fake, rude, chemically unstable way.
## Field ↔ figure relationship
One of the most important rules in the entire repo:
Abstract fields should be allowed to:
- birth figures
- absorb figures
- hint at hidden figures
- mimic body shapes
- become emblems
- serve as pre-anatomical material
Examples:
- contour field revealing a face
- rainbow melt birthing an eye
- thermal bands turning into a mouth cavity
- plasma blob becoming a little creature
- topographic abstraction aligning into a hand glyph
## Animation behavior
These fields often animate beautifully because they are naturally compatible with:
- morphing
- flow
- threshold changes
- domain warp
- palette cycling
- pressure shifts
- emergence and receding
- recursive loop logic
This makes them ideal for GLSL and generative systems.
## Good emotional range
Abstract fields can feel:
- dreamy
- ominous
- chemically ecstatic
- dumbly cosmic
- fake-deep

- grossly beautiful
- trance-like
- playful
- unstable
- charged
## What to avoid
### Too generic
- default rainbow fractal slop
- generic “trippy background”
- meaningless AI color mush
- overly smooth wallpaper abstraction
### Too noble
- polished spiritual vortex imagery
- majestic aura art
- elegant cosmic gradients with no weirdness
### Too detached
- abstract fields that do not feel related to the repo’s bodily, symbolic, or mutational logic
## Prompt guidance
Good phrases include:
- contour-band hallucination field
- cheap psychedelic topography
- liquid false topology
- thermal rainbow melt
- shader-like plasma distortion
- synthetic portal field
- flat acid contour map
- bodily hallucination substrate
- cartoon cosmological signal soup
## Final rule
Abstract hallucination fields should not just decorate the style.
They should feel like:
**the visual soup from which the symbols, creatures, and body glyphs are constantly trying to
crawl out**

STYLE_BOUNDARIES.md
# STYLE BOUNDARIES
This document defines what belongs inside Garbage Enlightenment and what falls outside it.
The style is flexible, but not infinite.
If boundaries are not stated clearly, models and humans alike will start drifting into neighboring
styles that share a few traits but miss the actual nerve.
## Core test
An image belongs if it strongly preserves most of these:
- flat cartoon simplification
- rude outlined graphic language
- bodily glyph logic
- sweet corruption or cute contamination
- synthetic acid color behavior
- mock-esoteric or symbolic charge
- mutational instability
- figure–pattern permeability
- low-fi or anti-polish energy
- prankster / fake-serious / weird emotional tone
If too many of those disappear, the image becomes another thing.
## Strongly inside the style
### Definitely belongs
- floating eyes, mouths, hands, skulls used as symbols
- flat mutant creatures with body weirdness
- cute motifs contaminated by mutation
- occult-looking icons treated with irony or absurdity
- fast morph animation where identities slide into each other
- psychedelic contour abstractions that feel shader-like
- cheap cosmic iconography
- rude, simple, synthetic cartoon weirdness
- images that feel like stickers from a deranged revelation pamphlet
## Borderline but potentially valid
### May belong if treated correctly
- kawaii-adjacent motifs

- dark horror imagery
- visionary-looking fields
- retro digital glitch
- childish or toy-like imagery
- psychedelic abstraction
- ritual symbols
- fantasy animals
- skull graphics
These only belong if they are sufficiently filtered through the repo’s logic.
## Outside the style
### 1. Polished visionary mysticism
This includes:
- reverent spiritual art
- noble aura paintings
- beautiful sacred geometry without cartoon corruption
- profound-seeking transcendence imagery played fully straight
Why it falls outside:
Too sincere, too clean, too elevated, too lacking in prankster bodily weirdness.
--## 2. Clean kawaii / mascot sweetness
This includes:
- cute characters with no contamination
- pristine sticker mascots
- polished adorable icon systems
- soft, innocent charm with no wrongness
Why it falls outside:
Too pure, too resolved, too nice, too lacking in mutation and symbolic instability.
--## 3. Grim realistic body horror
This includes:
- gore-heavy work
- anatomical horror
- sadistic realism
- visceral flesh realism
- clinical disgust imagery

Why it falls outside:
Too brutal, too serious, too literal, too lacking in cartoon abstraction.
--## 4. Corporate playful illustration
This includes:
- editorial flat vector style
- startup-friendly weirdness
- playful blob illustration with no symbolic charge
- modern clean design language
Why it falls outside:
Too clean, too functional, too polite, too dead.
--## 5. Ornate psychedelic poster classicism
This includes:
- heavy filigree
- Art Nouveau ornamental psychedelia
- lettering-centered 60s poster flourishes
- highly decorative poster symmetry
Why it falls outside:
Related historically in a broad sense, but too ornate and elegant for this repo’s rude mutant
cartoon identity.
--## 6. Generic AI “trippy rainbow” slop
This includes:
- default rainbow fractals
- over-rendered visionary mush
- hyper-detailed glowing surrealism
- symmetrical psychedelic wallpaper
Why it falls outside:
Too generic, too grandiose, too polished, too lacking specific motif intelligence.
--## 7. Serious occult branding

This includes:
- crisp eye-in-triangle logos
- sleek black-and-gold esoteric merch aesthetics
- polished sigil systems
- solemn secret-order design
Why it falls outside:
Too cool, too resolved, too committed to its own authority.
## Internal range boundaries
The style can move around internally, but these ranges should stay anchored.
### Cute range
Valid, but must retain at least one of:
- mutation
- bodily glyph
- rude line quality
- symbolic weirdness
- occult parody
- semantic wrongness
### Horror range
Valid, but should avoid:
- gore realism
- cruelty-first tone
- grim solemnity
- fully serious monster aesthetics
### Abstract range
Valid, but must feel tied to:
- mutation
- hallucination
- symbolic emergence
- cheap shader logic
- figure potential
### Occult range
Valid, but should avoid:
- too much reverence
- too much polish
- fully doctrinal seriousness
## Quick acceptance questions

Ask:
### Does it feel too clean?
If yes, probably drifting.
### Does it feel too serious?
If yes, probably drifting.
### Does it feel too pretty in a noble way?
If yes, probably drifting.
### Does it feel too cute without contamination?
If yes, probably drifting.
### Does it feel too gross without playfulness?
If yes, probably drifting.
### Does it feel like it could morph into something else?
If no, probably drifting.
### Does it have any symbolic bodily intelligence?
If no, probably drifting.
## Final boundary statement
Garbage Enlightenment lives in a specific zone:
Not fully cute.
Not fully dark.
Not fully sacred.
Not fully stupid.
Not fully abstract.
Not fully figurative.
Not fully serious.
Not fully decorative.
It should always feel like it is hovering in an unstable but intentional middle state.
That instability is the border fence.

ANTI_DRIFT.md
# ANTI-DRIFT
This file exists to stop Garbage Enlightenment from sliding into nearby styles that share some
surface traits but miss the actual style logic.
Drift is not random.
It tends to happen in a few predictable directions.
This document names those failure modes and how to correct them.
## Main principle
When the style drifts, it usually becomes:
- too polished
- too serious
- too generic
- too cute
- too dark
- too static
- too detached from bodily symbolism
- too detached from metamorphosis
The fix is usually to restore:
- rude simplicity
- body glyph logic
- transformability
- parody charge
- sweet corruption
- cheap synthetic weirdness
## Drift mode 1: TOO POLISHED
### Symptoms
- clean vector design
- sleek rendering
- corporate flatness
- refined gradients
- elegant finish
- smooth contemporary editorial look
### Why it fails
The style needs friction, cheapness, and a little disrespect.

Over-polish kills the voltage.
### Corrections
- roughen outlines
- flatten forms
- simplify anatomy
- reduce rendering
- make shapes ruder
- introduce asymmetry
- add one symbolic body fragment
- restore low-fi digital or hand-done energy
--## Drift mode 2: TOO SERIOUS / TOO SACRED
### Symptoms
- noble occult imagery
- sincere mystical reverence
- majestic cosmic beauty
- profound spiritual tone
- overly symmetrical sacred forms
### Why it fails
The style thrives on mock seriousness, prankster charge, and symbolic instability.
### Corrections
- add a bodily mutation
- make one symbol ridiculous
- reduce visual sanctity
- add a cute contaminant
- make the linework cheaper
- break the composition slightly
- turn one emblem into a creature or mouth
--## Drift mode 3: TOO CUTE / TOO PURE
### Symptoms
- adorable mascots
- polished sweet sticker art
- harmless charm
- toy design with no corruption

- kawaii without wrongness
### Why it fails
Sweetness alone is not enough.
This style needs contamination, mutation, or symbolic weirdness.
### Corrections
- add bodily glyphs
- insert asymmetry
- add a weird semantic substitution
- put an eye where it should not be
- make a cute motif a little wrong
- introduce occult parody or fake-serious symbolism
- roughen the line quality
--## Drift mode 4: TOO DARK / TOO HORROR-FIRST
### Symptoms
- grim monsters
- realistic gore
- heavy body horror
- sadistic or oppressive tone
- no playfulness
### Why it fails
This style can be ominous, but it should usually preserve some absurdity, sweetness, or cartoon
distance.
### Corrections
- brighten symbolic contrast
- add stars, hearts, rainbows, or cute creature energy
- simplify anatomy
- make the horror more comic or toy-like
- reduce realism
- restore playful or fake-sacred tension
--## Drift mode 5: TOO ABSTRACT / UNRELATED
### Symptoms
- random psychedelic wallpaper

- generic color mush
- beautiful but disconnected abstract fields
- no sense of body, symbol, or emergence
### Why it fails
Abstract work in this repo should still feel like it could birth a figure or glyph.
### Corrections
- add field structures that imply emergence
- hint at eyes, mouths, hands, or contour bodies
- use stronger contour logic
- make the field feel like mutation substrate
- connect it to symbolic use
--## Drift mode 6: TOO STATIC
### Symptoms
- stable posing
- clean emblem with no mutational tension
- image feels fixed and finalized
- no sense that the form could become something else
### Why it fails
Even still images should imply morphability.
### Corrections
- add asymmetrical pressure
- suggest split identity
- embed secondary forms
- use repeated motifs
- let one shape hint at another
- treat the image like a paused mutation, not a finished emblem
--## Drift mode 7: TOO GENERIC “TRIPPY”
### Symptoms
- rainbow overload with no structure
- AI surreal mush
- default fractal vibes
- symmetrical mandala drift

- ornamental psychedelia with no rude cartoon intelligence
### Why it fails
This style is specific.
It is not just “psychedelic.”
### Corrections
- reduce ornamental noise
- introduce body glyphs
- restore cartoon simplification
- use a clear symbolic subject
- make the weirdness feel chosen, not automatic
- bring back rude linework and fake-esoteric comedy
--## Drift mode 8: TOO COOL
### Symptoms
- crisp underground brand look
- stylish darkness
- detached graphic confidence
- hip design energy instead of mutant instability
### Why it fails
The style should be a little embarrassing, a little bodily, a little pathetic, or a little too much.
### Corrections
- make it less composed
- make it more bodily
- add soft stupidity
- weaken coolness with awkwardness
- allow sweetness or grossness to break the pose
## Emergency recovery checklist
If an image feels wrong, ask:
### 1. Where is the bodily glyph?
Need at least one clear body-symbol anchor.
### 2. Where is the contamination?
Need at least one element that corrupts purity, polish, or coherence.

### 3. Where is the cheapness?
Need some anti-luxury, anti-corporate, anti-overfinished quality.
### 4. Where is the transformability?
Need a sense that the image is mid-becoming.
### 5. Where is the tonal friction?
Need cute vs gross, sacred vs stupid, symbol vs body, or figure vs pattern tension.
## Prompt rescue phrases
Useful rescue language:
- rude outlined cartoon mutation
- low-fi symbolic body weirdness
- fake-sacred acid icon
- cute motif contaminated by bodily mutation
- cheap hallucination field
- figure dissolving into contour map
- prankster occult sticker logic
- unstable symbolic metamorphosis
- half-formed transition state
- goofy cosmic dread
## Final anti-drift law
When in doubt, do not ask:
“how can this be prettier?”
Ask:
**“how can this be slightly more wrong while still being legible?”**
That usually brings it home.
