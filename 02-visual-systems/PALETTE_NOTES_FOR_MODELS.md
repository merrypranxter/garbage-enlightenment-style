# PALETTE NOTES FOR MODELS

This file explains how image models, prompt systems, and generative tools should interpret
color inside Garbage Enlightenment.

The goal is not just to list colors.
The goal is to explain how color behaves.

## Core principle

In Garbage Enlightenment, color is not merely decorative.
It helps carry:
- symbolic pressure
- tonal instability
- bodily implication
- contamination
- fake revelation energy
- cheap synthetic charge

Models should not interpret the palette as:
- tasteful harmony
- luxury surreal beauty
- generic psychedelic rainbowing
- polished atmospheric glow

They should interpret it as:
- synthetic
- emotionally overcommitted
- symbolically rude
- chemically fake
- sometimes sweet
- sometimes body-adjacent

- often slightly wrong

## KEY MODEL INSTRUCTIONS

### 1. Favor flat or semi-flat color logic
Do not over-render surfaces into painterly realism unless explicitly needed.

### 2. Keep black or dark outlines structurally important
The outline anchors the chaos.

### 3. Let color zones feel chosen, not naturally lit
This style often behaves like:
- stickers
- icons
- pressure maps
- contour bands
- body signs

### 4. Preserve contrast between cute and bodily tones
Examples:
- candy pink vs cavity black
- rainbow arc vs mouth red
- star yellow vs bruise mauve
- toy blue vs weird peach flesh

### 5. Avoid smoothing everything into dreamy gradient mush
This kills the style fast.

## PALETTE FUNCTION TYPES

### Signal colors
Used to attract immediate attention.
Examples:
- hot pink
- acid yellow
- electric cyan
- hard red

### Body colors
Used to remind the style of flesh, cavities, or bodily presence.
Examples:
- weird peach
- tongue pink
- bruise mauve
- tooth red
- bone cream

### Cute colors

Used in Sweet Corruption mode.
Examples:
- candy pink
- sticker lavender
- sweet sky blue
- toy teal
- rainbow accents

### Gross/toxic accents
Used to prevent purity and prettiness.
Examples:
- mold green
- bile yellow
- rot green
- sour orange
- dirty support neutrals

## MODEL FAILURE RISKS

### Failure 1: Too smooth
The model turns the palette into polished gradient fantasy.

### Fix
Prompt for:

- flat cartoon chroma
- rude synthetic color blocks
- poster-like color zoning
- black-outlined forms

### Failure 2: Too generic rainbow
The model floods the image with undifferentiated psychedelic spectrum noise.

### Fix
Prompt for:
- body-color interruptions
- contour-band fields
- symbolic color hierarchy
- sweet-to-toxic contrast

### Failure 3: Too tasteful
The model harmonizes colors into something elegant and boring.

### Fix
Prompt for:
- chemically fake color
- rude color clashes
- candy-toxic contrast
- emotionally unstable palette behavior

### Failure 4: Too dark and muddy
The model leans horror or “serious surrealism.”

### Fix
Reintroduce:
- acid yellows
- toy brights
- candy accents
- sweet motifs
- fake revelation contrast spikes

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

When the style leans into its **cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic**, color should often behave
like:
- significance has attached itself to hue boundaries
- contour transitions feel emotionally active
- a color change implies a meaning shift
- rainbow behavior carries symbolic charge rather than mere decoration

That does **not** mean “more rainbow everywhere.”
It means the palette should feel:
- pressurized
- suggestive

- over-meaningful
- unstable in a readable way

## STRONG MODEL PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- synthetic acid cartoon palette
- rude candy-toxic color logic
- flesh tones colliding with neon contour bands
- flat hallucinatory chroma zoning
- body-haunted rainbow pressure
- cheap symbolic color contrast
- chemically fake palette with bodily interruptions

## FINAL RULE

A model should render Garbage Enlightenment color like:
**a synthetic emotional pressure system, not like tasteful lighting**
