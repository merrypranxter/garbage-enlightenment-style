# CONTOUR BAND NOTES

This file focuses specifically on contour-band logic in Garbage Enlightenment.

Contour bands are one of the style’s strongest abstract tools because they can act as:
- topography
- aura
- body pressure
- emergence substrate
- symbolic weather
- fake sacred radiance
- contour logic for morphing

## Core principle

Contour bands should not just be:
- pretty stripes

- decorative psychedelia
- thermal wallpaper
- smooth topographic design

They should feel like:
- pressure
- emergence
- body memory
- symbolic zoning
- instability under simple structure

## MAIN CONTOUR BAND FUNCTIONS

---

## 1. FIELD PRESSURE

Contour bands create the sense that the image is under:
- emotional compression
- symbolic tension
- hallucination pressure
- fake revelation gravity

This helps otherwise simple icons feel more alive.

---

## 2. EMERGENCE SUPPORT

Contour bands are excellent for:
- hidden face logic
- eye emergence
- hand formation
- creature implication
- field-to-figure transitions

---

## 3. BODY ANALOGUE

Contour bands can suggest:
- flesh regions
- pressure zones
- soft anatomy
- body topography
- cavity mapping

This helps abstraction stay body-haunted.

---

## 4. HALO / PORTAL SUBSTITUTE

A contour band cluster can replace or complicate a simple halo ring.

This gives:
- more instability
- more body pressure
- more LSD-logic implication
- less polished sacredness

## SHAPE BEHAVIOR

Good contour bands often:
- wobble
- drift
- warp
- squeeze
- separate
- merge
- imply hidden structures
- act like they want to resolve into something

Bad contour bands often:
- remain too decorative
- become too smooth
- stay too neutral
- fail to imply any symbolic pressure

## COLOR LOGIC

Best contour-band color modes:
- thermal revelation
- native acid
- sweet toxin in softer cases
- bruise rainbow for eerie field pressure

Good contour-band colors should feel:
- synthetic
- pressure-mapped
- emotionally active
- slightly rude

## CONTOUR BANDS + FACE PRESSURE

This is a major combo.

Contour bands can:
- imply eye sockets
- imply mouth seam
- imply cheek plane
- imply head silhouette
- support recursive face logic

This is one of the strongest bridges between abstraction and figure.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Contour bands are one of the most efficient tools for encoding **cartoonized LSD-hallucination
logic** because they let:
- one shape suggest multiple readings
- significance attach to color boundaries
- pattern become anatomy
- anatomy become pattern
- figures appear as if the field was already thinking them
