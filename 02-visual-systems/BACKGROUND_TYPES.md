# BACKGROUND TYPES

This file describes background strategies used in Garbage Enlightenment.

Backgrounds are not just empty space-fillers.
They affect:
- symbolic charge
- tonal balance
- figure readability
- hallucination pressure
- composition logic
- motion potential

## Core principle

A background in this style should usually do one or more of the following:
- isolate the symbol
- contaminate the figure
- pressure the figure

- imply emergence
- soften or intensify tone
- support fake revelation
- add sweet decorative contrast
- behave like a hallucination field

## MAIN BACKGROUND MODES

---

## 1. FLAT COLOR VOID

### Description
A single-color or near-single-color flat background.

### Use
- isolates a central icon
- strengthens sticker logic
- supports poster readability
- makes body glyphs feel detached and symbolic

### Best for
- eye icons
- hand symbols

- floating heads
- simple creature portraits

### Good tones
- acid yellow
- weird peach
- hot pink
- toxic green
- dirty cream
- flat cyan
- body-neutral support colors

---

## 2. DECORATIVE SYMBOL FIELD

### Description
A flat background populated by small repeated motifs:
- stars
- hearts
- bubbles
- eyes
- little glyphs
- sparkles

### Use
- Sweet Corruption
- fake revelation clutter
- dream sticker vibe
- playfulness

### Best for
- cute/gross mixtures
- emblematic icons
- creature compositions

---

## 3. CONTOUR-BAND FIELD

### Description
A background of topographic / warped contour bands / psychedelic isolines.

### Use
- LSD-logic pressure
- figure emergence
- fake sacred aura
- abstract subsystem integration

### Best for
- hands
- eyes
- face mutations
- figure-pattern hybrids
- shader testing

---

## 4. THERMAL / OIL-SLICK FIELD

### Description
A rainbow pressure-map or chemically unstable synthetic field.

### Use
- abstract hallucination mode
- emotional overcharge
- body-haunted backdrop
- fake cosmic drama

### Best for
- hybrid images
- motion-heavy concepts

- portal imagery
- creatures dissolving into fields

---

## 5. EMPTY STAGE SPACE

### Description
A mostly blank stage-like background with slight tonal hinting.

### Use
- keeps focus on the creature or symbol
- supports awkward, emblematic staging
- increases patheticness or vulnerability

### Best for
- small weird creatures
- face-creatures
- symbolic portraits

---

## 6. SIGNAL DAMAGE BACKGROUND

### Description
A background with:
- dither
- subtle digital noise
- scanline hints
- shimmer
- cheap digital artifacts

### Use
- anti-polish support
- low-fi digital atmosphere
- slight video / GIF / cheap-screen feeling

### Best for
- abstract subsystem
- loop prompts
- digital-emphasis outputs

---

## 7. HYBRID FIELD BACKGROUND

### Description
A background that starts as abstract field and partially resolves into symbolic motifs.

Examples:
- contour bands with hidden eyes
- rainbow field with tiny mouths
- stars emerging from plasma
- halo rings forming from heatmap edges

### Use
- strongest high-identity outputs
- style-defining work
- figure-pattern permeability

## BACKGROUND FUNCTION MODES

A background may function as:

### 1. Isolation
Makes the symbol pop.

### 2. Contamination
Leaks weirdness into the figure.

### 3. Emergence substrate
Feels like the figure could have formed out of it.

### 4. Tone regulator
Makes the image sweeter, grosser, or more ominous.

### 5. Recurrence field
Supports repeated motifs and loop logic.

## WHAT TO AVOID

### Too realistic
- deep scenic perspective
- detailed environment realism
- naturalistic space-building

### Too empty
- blankness with no symbolic charge

### Too decorative in the wrong way
- elegant wallpaper
- tasteful ornamental design
- generic “pretty background”

### Too generic psychedelic
- default rainbow mush

- soft cosmic blur
- majestic spiritual nebula vibes

## FINAL RULE

A Garbage Enlightenment background should feel like:
**either a flat symbolic stage, a decorative contamination field, or a body-haunted hallucination
substrate**

Nothing should feel merely neutral.
