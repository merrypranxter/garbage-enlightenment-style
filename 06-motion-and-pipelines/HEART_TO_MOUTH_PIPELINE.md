# HEART TO MOUTH PIPELINE

This file focuses on another core Sweet Corruption transformation:

**heart → mouth**

This is one of the style’s most useful cute-to-bodily conversions.

The heart begins as:
- sweet
- decorative
- symbolic affection
- toy logic
- sticker energy

The mouth introduces:
- body weirdness
- opening logic
- humor
- threat
- threshold behavior
- fake intimacy
- grossness

When the heart becomes a mouth, the style wakes right the fuck up.

## Core principle

A heart-to-mouth conversion should feel:

- simple but weirdly potent
- emotionally unstable
- cute and bodily at once
- a little fake sacred if desired
- visually blunt but semantically slippery

## PIPELINE STAGES

---

## STAGE 1 — PURE HEART

Start with:
- classic heart silhouette
- decorative sticker heart
- candy icon heart
- soft heart badge

At this stage it is still sweet, portable, and emotionally simple.

---

## STAGE 2 — SEAM PRESSURE

The heart begins to imply:
- a crack
- a seam
- a split
- a mouth line
- a body opening

This can be subtle and is often a very strong still-image state.

---

## STAGE 3 — HEART MOUTH HINT

Now the heart can be read two ways:
- a heart
- a mouth
- a cavity sign

Good features:
- lower cleft acting like lip logic
- dark line/seam
- slight asymmetry
- tooth hints
- contour bulge

---

## STAGE 4 — HEART MOUTH ICON

The transformation becomes explicit.

Forms may include:
- teeth in the center
- a black mouth cavity
- tongue logic
- lips implied by heart edges

At this stage it becomes:
- Sweet Corruption icon
- excellent sticker badge
- fake intimacy symbol
- soft menace emblem

---

## STAGE 5 — HEART AS BODY OPENING

The heart is now less “cute heart” and more:

- body sign
- symbolic cavity
- emotional opening
- portal-mouth-adjacent emblem

It may still retain:
- cute silhouette memory
- decorative framing
- stars/hearts support motifs

## GOOD SUPPORT ELEMENTS

- stars
- aura rings
- rainbow arcs
- eyes
- contour bands
- little tears/drips
- fake sacred framing

## GOOD VARIANTS

### Heart with teeth
Classic Sweet Corruption.

### Heart with single eye and mouth seam
Cute + watchful + bodily.

### Haloed heart-mouth
Fake revelation tenderness.

### Rainbow heart-mouth
Decorative LSD-logic bridge.

### Blob-heart mouth
More vulnerable and gross-soft.

## BAD VARIANTS

### Too Valentine's
Becomes commercial romance design.

### Too polished
Becomes premium cute merch.

### Too gory
Loses cartoon body weirdness.

### Too neat
Stops feeling mutational.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

This pipeline strongly reflects **cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic** because:
- a cute symbol becomes anatomy without fully losing its original role
- emotional meaning and bodily meaning collapse into one icon
- one silhouette carries several jobs at once
- significance feels amplified by symbolic compression

This is a perfect example of the repo’s method.
