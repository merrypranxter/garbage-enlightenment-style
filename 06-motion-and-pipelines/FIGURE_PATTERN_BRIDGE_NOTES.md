# FIGURE PATTERN BRIDGE NOTES

This file focuses specifically on one of the most important laws in Garbage Enlightenment:

**figure and pattern should be able to become each other**

This is one of the style’s strongest engines and one of its clearest links to cartoonized
LSD-hallucination logic.

## Core principle

The style is strongest when:
- figures do not feel fully separate from fields
- symbols can emerge from abstraction
- creatures can dissolve into contour systems
- pattern behaves like latent anatomy
- anatomy behaves like decorative structure

## WHY THIS MATTERS

Without figure-pattern bridge logic, the style can split into two weaker halves:
- generic creature cartooning
- generic psychedelic abstraction

The bridge is what fuses them into one organism.

## MAIN BRIDGE MODES

---

## 1. EMERGENCE FROM FIELD

A readable figure or glyph appears from:
- contour bands
- thermal maps
- plasma blobs
- noise fields
- abstract color pressure

Examples:
- face emerging from rainbow field
- hand revealed inside contour system
- eye appearing from topographic void

---

## 2. DISSOLUTION INTO FIELD

A figure loses identity and slips into:
- heatmap regions
- decorative repetition
- contour bands
- signal soup
- body-haunted abstraction

Examples:
- creature becoming contour weather
- mouth fading into dark pressure map
- head dissolving into plasma

---

## 3. PARTIAL READABILITY

The bridge is often strongest when the image is not fully one thing or the other.

Examples:

- almost-face
- almost-hand
- almost-creature
- pattern that still feels bodily
- field that still feels like it remembers the head it came from

This is where meaningful intermediate states live.

---

## 4. DECORATIVE ANATOMY

Body parts become pattern units.

Examples:
- repeating eye pattern
- mouths as border motif
- little skulls in decorative field
- teeth turned into ornament

---

## 5. PATTERNED CREATUREHOOD

Abstract rhythm or pattern structure begins to imply creature logic.

Examples:
- contour bands acting like limbs
- plasma blobs turning into swarm-beasts
- repeated motifs behaving like distributed consciousness

## USEFUL PROMPT PHRASES

- figure-pattern permeability
- body-haunted abstract field
- latent face emergence
- contour bands resolving into creature logic
- pattern leaking into anatomy
- anatomy flattening into decorative signal
- half-symbol half-field
- paused between icon and hallucination map

## COMMON FAILURE MODES

### Failure 1
Figure and background are too separate.

### Fix

Make the field actively contain or leak the figure.

### Failure 2
Abstract area feels empty and decorative only.

### Fix
Add emergence pressure or body-glyph implication.

### Failure 3
Figure is too stable and self-contained.

### Fix
Let edges dissolve, repeat, or imply other readings.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

This bridge is one of the clearest places where the style resembles **cartoonized
LSD-hallucination logic**:
- figure/ground instability
- one thing implying another
- patterns becoming meaningful entities
- entities reabsorbed by visual texture

Again, the repo is not trying to simulate literal psychophysics.
It is translating that unstable logic into rude symbolic cartoon space.

## FINAL RULE

A strong Garbage Enlightenment image often feels like:
**the pattern wants to become a body and the body is already halfway back to pattern**
