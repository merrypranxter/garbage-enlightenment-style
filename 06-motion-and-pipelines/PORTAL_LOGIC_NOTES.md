# PORTAL LOGIC NOTES

This file explains portal logic in Garbage Enlightenment.

Portals in this style are not always literal sci-fi gates.
They are often:
- symbolic openings
- mouth-cavities
- halo-rings
- eye interiors
- contour tunnels
- fake transcendence devices
- decorative thresholds
- body-sign voids

## Core principle

A portal should often feel like:

- a threshold
- an opening
- a fake revelation site
- a symbolic mouth
- a cheap cosmic passage
- a body hole pretending to be metaphysical

That body/threshold ambiguity is one of the style’s strongest flavors.

## MAIN PORTAL TYPES

---

## 1. HALO PORTAL

### Description
A ring or aura frame that behaves as both:
- sacred icon support
and
- threshold

### Best use
- behind heads
- around hands

- framing an eye
- supporting a central glyph

### Tone
- fake holy
- symbolic
- slightly overcommitted
- not fully trustworthy

---

## 2. MOUTH PORTAL

### Description
A mouth or cavity that behaves like a threshold rather than just anatomy.

### Best use
- body-heavy imagery
- Sweet Corruption
- face mutations
- fake revelation body logic

### Tone
- gross-funny

- bodily
- uncanny
- over-significant

---

## 3. EYE PORTAL

### Description
The eye acts as:
- tunnel
- opening
- threshold
- cosmic focal point
- seeing-hole

### Best use
- revelation icons
- face symbolism
- abstract emergence
- loop animations

### Tone
- watchful

- fake profound
- pressure-heavy
- symbolically loaded

---

## 4. CONTOUR PORTAL

### Description
A portal made from abstract field behavior:
- contour rings
- false topography
- thermal circles
- warped zones

### Best use
- field-heavy work
- shader prompts
- abstract hybrids

### Tone
- body-haunted abstraction

- LSD-logic field pressure

## 5. BADGE PORTAL

### Description
A portal compressed into a sticker/emblem/badge shape.

### Best use
- icon sheets
- posters
- fake sacred logos
- graphic systems

### Tone
- symbolic
- portable
- fake-significant
- low-budget holy

## PORTAL RULES

### Rule 1

A portal should carry symbolic charge, not just depth.

### Rule 2
A portal should usually relate to:
- body
- symbol
- revelation
- contamination
- pattern pressure

### Rule 3
A portal can be cute, gross, eerie, or fake-sacred.
But it should not become generic fantasy VFX.

## PORTAL + LSD-LOGIC

Portal logic becomes strongly Garbage Enlightenment-specific when:
- the portal could also be a body opening
- the threshold is semantically unstable
- the ring/void feels over-significant
- a simple opening implies too many possible meanings at once

This is one of the best ways to encode cartoonized LSD-hallucination structure without drifting
into polished spiritual art.

## PROMPT PHRASES

- mouth-like portal

## FINAL RULE

A good Garbage Enlightenment portal should feel like:
**a body problem and a metaphysical event at the same time**
