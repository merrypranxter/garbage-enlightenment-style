# CUTE NECROSIS NOTES

This file focuses on the Cute Necrosis branch inside Garbage Enlightenment.

Cute Necrosis is the zone where:
- skulls
- bones
- death symbols
- empty sockets
- little relics
- eerie remains

meet:
- sweetness
- decorative softness
- toy logic
- sticker charm
- fake sacred framing

- gentle wrongness

## Core principle

Cute Necrosis is not:
- grim death art
- goth branding
- horror realism
- edgy dark-cute cliché

It is:
- soft death
- symbolic mortality made decorative
- eerie sweetness
- affectionate wrongness
- death as sticker logic
- body emptiness turned into ornament

## WHAT MAKES IT WORK

Cute Necrosis works when death motifs feel:
- iconically simple
- low-threat
- emotionally mixed

- a little playful
- a little sad
- a little fake-profound
- capable of being adorable and eerie at once

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Cute Necrosis gets more specific when:
- a skull seems almost sweet enough to be a toy

- death symbols feel over-significant and emotionally unstable
