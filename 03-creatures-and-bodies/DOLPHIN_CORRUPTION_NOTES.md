# DOLPHIN CORRUPTION NOTES

This file focuses on one of the weirder and more useful Sweet Corruption motifs in Garbage
Enlightenment:

**the dolphin**

The dolphin is useful because it begins with:
- sweetness
- toy-animal familiarity
- fake spiritual innocence
- decorative friendliness
- soft 80s/90s symbolic optimism

and can be pushed toward:
- fake sacred weirdness
- body-glyph contamination
- symbolic instability
- cute menace
- pathetic creaturehood
- over-significant mascot logic

## Core principle

A Garbage Enlightenment dolphin should not remain:
- generic cute dolphin clipart
- pure New Age sweetness
- clean Lisa-Frank-adjacent fantasy-animal decoration
- polished “spiritual animal” illustration

It should feel like:
- a sweet icon with symbolic contamination
- a cute creature under weird emotional pressure
- a fake spiritual mascot with a body problem
- a decorative animal trying too hard to mean something

## WHY DOLPHINS WORK

Dolphins are strong because they already carry:
- fake transcendence
- soft mystical cliché energy

- toy-symbol readability
- instant sweetness
- pop-cultural spiritual silliness

That makes them perfect for:
- parody
- Sweet Corruption
- fake revelation
- mascot destabilization
