# CREATURE NAMING LOGIC

This file proposes naming strategies for creatures and recurring beings inside Garbage
Enlightenment.

The goal is not fantasy lore seriousness.
The goal is names that support the style’s tone:

- weird
- symbolic
- a little pathetic
- a little fake-sacred
- a little cute
- a little gross
- slightly embarrassing in a good way

## Core principle

Creature names should usually feel like they came from:
- body logic
- symbolic behavior
- emotional wrongness
- mutation status
- decorative identity
- fake revelation tone

They should not sound too:
- epic
- macho
- fantasy-novel serious
- overcool
- generic monster-lore

## GOOD NAME TYPES

---

## 1. BODY + MOOD

Examples:
- Gum Wailer
- Tongue Angel
- Eye Baby
- Mouth Sprite
- Teeth Bloom
- Palm Ghost
- Skull Cherub
- Blob Weeper

Why it works:
Simple, symbolic, emotionally immediate.

---

## 2. CUTE + WRONG

Examples:
- Rainbow Rotter
- Star Lump
- Heart Goblin
- Dolphin Maw
- Cloud Teeth
- Candy Skull
- Bubble Wound
- Soft Menace

Why it works:
Preserves Sweet Corruption.

---

## 3. FAKE SACRED + PATHETIC

Examples:
- Little Prophet Thing
- Portal Idiot
- Halo Lump
- Saint Blobby
- Revelation Goblin
- Palm Oracle

- Cheap Seraph
- Warning Baby

Why it works:
Hits Mock Revelation without becoming too grand.

---

## 4. ABSTRACT + CREATURE

Examples:
- Contour Rat
- Plasma Pup
- Thermal Freak
- Bandface
- Signal Toad
- Topography Mouth
- Drift Beast
- Static Cherub

Why it works:
Bridges abstraction and creaturehood.

## NAMING FORMULAS

### Formula A
[body part] + [creature type]
Examples:
- Eye Toad
- Mouth Pup
- Skull Worm

### Formula B
[cute motif] + [gross/body word]
Examples:
- Rainbow Maw
- Star Rot
- Heart Lump

### Formula C
[fake sacred term] + [pathetic form]
Examples:
- Budget Seraph
- Halo Goblin
- Portal Weirdo

### Formula D
[field term] + [body term]

Examples:
- Contour Skull
- Plasma Tongue
- Thermal Palm

### Formula E
[emotional adjective] + [symbolic noun]
Examples:
- Nervous Oracle
- Soft Warning
- Dumb Halo
- Tender Menace

## NAME TONE GUIDELINES

Good names can be:
- funny
- blunt
- sweetly wrong
- low-rent mystical
- body-aware
- weirdly affectionate
- slightly humiliating
- emotionally vivid

Bad names tend to be:
- dark-lord serious
- too metal
- too elegant
- too generic-fantasy
- too sci-fi-clean
- too lore-heavy

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Names can quietly support the style’s LSD-adjacent structure by implying:
- unstable role
- mixed symbolic identity
- body/symbol crossover
- over-significance

Examples:
- Eye Sermon
- Rainbow Oracle Mouth
- Halo Teeth
- Signal Cherub Blob

These feel like names from a world where meaning moves too fast, which is good.

## FINAL RULE

A good Garbage Enlightenment creature name should feel like:
**something between a sticker, a symptom, a pet name, a warning sign, and a hallucinated title
nobody fully earned**
