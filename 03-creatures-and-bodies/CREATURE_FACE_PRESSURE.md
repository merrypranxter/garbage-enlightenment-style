# CREATURE FACE PRESSURE

This file focuses on face pressure specifically inside creatures.

Creature face pressure is one of the strongest things in Garbage Enlightenment because
creatures often work best when they are:
- almost more face than body
- emotionally over-readable
- unstable in species identity
- carrying too much symbolic charge through the face

## Core principle

A good Garbage Enlightenment creature often feels like:
- a body built to support a face event
- a symbolic face with body consequences
- a creature whose personhood is too concentrated in the wrong place

The face is often the real main mass of the creature.

## WHY THIS WORKS

Face pressure inside creatures creates:
- immediate emotion
- pathos
- absurdity
- body weirdness
- species instability
- mascot corruption
- LSD-logic adjacency

## MAIN CREATURE FACE PRESSURE TYPES

---

## 1. OVERSIZED FACE DOMINANCE

### Description
The creature is mostly head/face, with minimal body.

### Effect
- patheticness
- vulnerability
- icon-like force

- emotional compression

---

## 2. FEATURE OVER-IMPORTANCE

### Description
One feature dominates:
- eye too large
- mouth too symbolic
- face seam too intense
- head contour too loaded

### Effect
- weird sincerity
- creature feels over-significant
- fake revelation creepiness

---

## 3. MULTIPLE FACIAL READINGS

### Description
The creature face can almost be read in more than one way.

### Effect
- identity instability
- symbolic over-meaning
- stronger LSD-logic structure

---

## 4. FACE BLEEDING INTO FIELD

### Description
The creature’s face starts to dissolve into:
- contour field
- decorative motifs
- body blobs
- little repeated faces

### Effect
- figure-pattern permeability
- emotional instability
- higher-style intensity

## GOOD FACE PRESSURE SUPPORTS

- aura rings
- contour fields
- stars/hearts
- repeated mini eyes
- asymmetry
- body-color interruptions
- fake sacred staging

## BAD CREATURE FACE PRESSURE

### Too portrait-like
Turns into character art in the wrong way.

### Too horror-realistic
Becomes monster face art.

### Too mascot-clean
Becomes dead branding.

### Too empty
Creature has no emotional center.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Creature face pressure is especially on-brand when:
- the creature seems like more than one being at once
- the face feels semantically overloaded
- expression and symbolism blur together
- one face keeps hinting at other possible faces inside it

This is a major structural echo of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- face-dominant creature
- too much face for one little body
- symbolic face pressure inside a tiny mutant creature
- over-significant facial creature logic
- pathetic being with unstable face identity

## FINAL RULE

A Garbage Enlightenment creature should often feel like:
**its face got there first and the rest of the creature had to improvise around it**
