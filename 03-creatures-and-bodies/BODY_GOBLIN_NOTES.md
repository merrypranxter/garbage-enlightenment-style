# BODY GOBLIN NOTES

This file focuses on one of the most alive and lovable sub-branches inside Garbage
Enlightenment:

the **Body Goblin**.

This is the zone of:
- pathetic little mutant beings
- soft body creatures
- face-creatures
- awkward symbolic beasts
- chemically dazed little freaks
- lovable weird anatomy

## Core principle

A Body Goblin should not feel:
- majestic
- badass
- hyper-competent
- cool in a detached way
- monster-design polished

It should feel like:
- it crawled out of a symbol by accident
- it is doing its best despite being made wrong
- it is half-body, half-cartoon idea
- it is weirdly sincere
- it might cry, bite, stare, wobble, or mutate at any time

## MAIN BODY GOBLIN TRAITS

### 1. Simplified anatomy
- stumpy limbs
- blob bodies
- awkward support structures
- big heads
- tiny feet
- unstable proportions

### 2. Symbolic facial logic
- eyes too large
- mouths too graphic
- features too close together or weirdly spaced
- one body glyph dominating the face

### 3. Pathetic posture
- sagging
- hunched
- wobbling
- slightly collapsed
- emotionally exposed

### 4. Anti-heroic energy
The creature should not read like:
- a fantasy monster boss
- premium character design
- cool mascot branding

It should read like:
- a creature who is trying
- a weird little being who has seen something
- a soft freak

- a body-made idiot angel
- a symbolic loser with charm

## BODY GOBLIN SUBTYPES

---

## 1. THE BLOB GOBLIN

### Traits
- mostly soft body mass
- minimal limbs
- eye-heavy face
- unstable silhouette
- high morph potential

### Emotional tone
- freak tenderness
- gross-soft
- pathetic charm

---

## 2. THE FACE GOBLIN

### Traits
- huge head
- body secondary or tiny
- facial instability
- expression-heavy
- body glyph logic obvious

### Emotional tone
- chemically stunned
- awkward
- over-meaningful
- toy-haunted

---

## 3. THE CUTE-CORRUPTED BEAST

### Traits
- animal-adjacent silhouette
- dolphin/dog/fish/unicorn-ish possibilities
- sweetness damaged by mutation
- symbolic eyes or mouth inserts

### Emotional tone
- cute absurdity
- soft eerie play
- lovable wrongness

---

## 4. THE SWARM GOBLIN

### Traits
- many small heads or blobs
- clustered consciousness
- repeated motifs
- distributed body logic

### Emotional tone
- silly dread
- hive weirdness
- decorative infestation

## BODY GOBLIN COLOR LOGIC

Best palettes:
- native acid

- dirt candy
- sweet toxin
- bruise rainbow

Useful color notes:
- body peach and weird pink help
- toxic or sour interruptions help
- cavity blacks and lip reds help
- cute accents can soften and complicate tone

## BODY GOBLIN MOTION LOGIC

These creatures should often move through:
- wobble
- pulse
- bulge
- leak
- split
- collapse
- semi-stable morphing
- blinking into other symbolic states

They often benefit from:
- cheap loop cadence

- expressive contour drift
- body-part reassignment
- awkward cyclical motion

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Body Goblins can strongly reflect **cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic** when:
- they imply more than one species at once
- their face keeps nearly becoming something else
- their form feels over-meaningful
- their body seems like a temporary agreement between several possible shapes

This is especially strong when the creature feels both:
- emotionally simple
and
- symbolically overloaded

## BODY GOBLIN FAILURE MODES

### Too cool
Becomes designer-toy creature branding.

### Too scary
Becomes horror monster.

### Too cute
Becomes mascot.

### Too random
Becomes meaningless weird blob.

### Too rendered
Becomes polished creature concept art.

## BODY GOBLIN REPAIR MOVES

- make the limbs worse
- make the face more symbolic
- add one sweet motif
- add one gross motif
- add asymmetry
- make the creature a little sadder or stupider
- reduce polish
- let it look underqualified for existence

## FINAL RULE

A great Body Goblin should feel like:

**a vulnerable little symbolic accident that somehow became a creature and now has to keep
being one**
