# IMAGE GEN RECIPES
These recipes are practical prompt structures for generating images in the Garbage
Enlightenment style.
They are designed to keep the style specific and prevent drift into:
- polished surrealism

- generic trippy AI mush
- corporate cute illustration
- solemn occult art
- grim body horror
- polished visionary art
## RECIPE 1 — SYMBOL POSTER
### Use for
- icon images
- style-defining single subjects
- strong readable outputs
### Formula
[main symbol or body glyph], in the Garbage Enlightenment / Sweet Corruption Acid Cartoon
Surrealism style, flat low-fi mutant cartoon drawing, rude black outlines, simplified graphic forms,
synthetic acid palette, fake-sacred sticker energy, slight bodily contamination, shallow symbolic
stage space, cute/gross/ominous tonal mixture, no polished realism, no solemn mysticism, no
clean corporate vector look
### Example structure
all-seeing eye inside a crude pyramid, flat low-fi mutant cartoon, rude black outlines, hot pink
and acid yellow palette, contour-band aura ring, fake revelation energy, slight mouth-like bodily
contamination, sweet corruption accents like stars and hearts, cheap symbolic poster
composition

## Prompting checklist
Before sending a prompt, make sure it contains:
- a subject
- a style anchor
- a line/shape anchor
- a tone anchor
- a symbolic system
- an anti-drift phrase
## Final rule
The strongest Garbage Enlightenment prompts usually combine:
- one symbolic anchor
- one body anchor
- one cute or decorative contaminant
- one abstract or hallucinatory subsystem
- one anti-polish instruction
