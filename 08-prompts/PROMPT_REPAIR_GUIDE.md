# PROMPT REPAIR GUIDE

This file helps repair prompts that drift away from Garbage Enlightenment.

Most weak prompts do not fail because they are too short.
They fail because they are too vague, too pure, too polished, or too generic.

## Core principle

A good repair process does not just “add more detail.”
It adds the **right structural detail**.

Specifically:
- body glyph anchors
- tone friction
- mutation logic
- anti-drift control
- symbolic specificity
- figure-pattern pressure

## COMMON BROKEN PROMPT TYPES

---

## 1. TOO GENERICALLY TRIPPY

### Broken prompt

“Make a trippy surreal psychedelic image with weird eyes and colors.”

### Problem
- too vague
- no body logic
- no tone logic
- no anti-drift control
- no style-specific weirdness

### Repair strategy
Add:
- rude black outlines
- body glyphs
- Sweet Corruption or mock esoterica
- cheap hallucination fields
- anti-polish control
- symbolic instability

### Repaired version
A low-fi mutant cartoon image in the Garbage Enlightenment style, with floating eye glyphs,
rude black outlines, synthetic acid color, contour-band hallucination fields, Sweet Corruption
stars and hearts, fake revelation energy, and body-symbol weirdness; avoid polished visionary
art and generic trippy rainbow slop

---

## 2. TOO CUTE

### Broken prompt
“A cute sticker sheet with stars, hearts, rainbows, and dolphins.”

### Problem
- too pure
- no contamination
- no body weirdness
- no symbolic instability

### Repair strategy
Add:
- eyes, mouths, or body glyphs
- asymmetry
- contamination language
- anti-pristine phrasing

### Repaired version
A Sweet Corruption sticker-sheet composition in the Garbage Enlightenment style, with stars,
hearts, rainbows, and dolphins contaminated by floating eyes, little mouths, body-weird
symbolic wrongness, rude black outlines, and synthetic candy-toxic color; avoid pristine kawaii
polish

---

## 3. TOO DARK / HORROR-FIRST

### Broken prompt
“A disturbing body horror monster with teeth and eyeballs.”

### Problem
- too grim
- too literal
- too horror-forward
- may drift into realistic gore

### Repair strategy
Add:
- cartoon simplification
- sweetness or fake revelation
- anti-gore language
- body-symbol framing rather than realistic anatomy

### Repaired version
A gross-funny mutant creature in the Garbage Enlightenment style, built from eyes, mouths, and
teeth, with rude black outlines, synthetic color, fake-sacred symbolic staging, and slight Sweet
Corruption accents; eerie and body-weird but not realistic gore, not grimdark, not polished
monster realism

---

## 4. TOO POLISHED / PRETTY

### Broken prompt
“A beautiful surreal psychedelic painting with detailed textures and glowing spiritual energy.”

### Problem
- too elegant
- too sincere
- too painterly
- too refined

### Repair strategy
Flatten it.
Cheapen it.
Add symbolic body logic.
Kill the sacred polish.

### Repaired version
A flat low-fi symbolic cartoon in the Garbage Enlightenment style, with rude black outlines, body
glyphs, fake revelation energy, contour-band hallucination fields, and anti-polish cheapness;
avoid beautiful spiritual painting, elegant surrealism, or polished transcendental glow

---

## 5. TOO ABSTRACT / EMPTY

### Broken prompt
“A psychedelic abstract field of rainbow flowing shapes.”

### Problem
- disconnected
- no motif pressure
- no emergence logic
- too generic

### Repair strategy
Make the field body-haunted.
Add emergence pressure.
Mention likely symbolic births.

### Repaired version
An abstract hallucination field in the Garbage Enlightenment style, with thermal rainbow contour
bands, threshold blob emergence, and the sense that eyes, mouths, or little creatures might
appear from the field at any moment; synthetic acid color, rude graphic pressure, no generic
trippy wallpaper drift

- avoid generic trippy slop

- cute absurdity
- freak tenderness
- gross-funny body weirdness

## LSD-REPAIR TOOL

If the prompt is supposed to feel “LSD-like” but doesn’t, do **not** just add:
- rainbow
- more eyes
- glow
- fractals

Instead add:
- cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic
- symbolic over-meaning
- rapid significance reassignment
- one form implying multiple forms
- figure-pattern instability
- contour-based metamorphic emergence

That will actually improve it.

## FINAL RULE

When repairing a weak prompt, ask:

**What actual engine of the style is missing?**

Usually the answer is one of:
- body glyphs
- contamination
- symbolic specificity
- tone friction
- mutation pressure
- anti-polish control

Fix that, and the prompt usually wakes back up.

# SUBSTYLE BRANCHES

This file maps the main substyle branches that naturally emerge inside Garbage Enlightenment.

These are not separate styles.
They are internal modes or emphasis clusters.

Each branch should still preserve the repo’s core laws:
- body glyph logic
- symbolic instability
- anti-polish energy
- Sweet Corruption or contamination logic
- figure-pattern permeability
- fake revelation / prankster symbolic charge
- some degree of mutational potential

## Core principle

A branch is valid when it changes emphasis without abandoning the style’s nervous system.

## 1. SWEET CORRUPTION BRANCH

### Main emphasis
- stars
- hearts
- rainbows
- dolphins
- clouds
- cute little creatures
- sticker logic
- toy sweetness

### Core tension
Cute motifs are contaminated by:
- body glyphs
- mouths
- eyes
- teeth
- asymmetry
- symbolic wrongness

### Best emotional range
- cute absurdity
- freak tenderness
- sugary wrongness

- soft eerie play

### Main drift risk
Turning into:
- clean kawaii
- polished sticker art
- harmless mascot design

### Fix
Reintroduce bodily contamination, low-fi linework, and symbolic instability.

---

## 2. MOCK REVELATION BRANCH

### Main emphasis
- all-seeing eyes
- pyramids
- hands
- portals
- aura rings
- fake sigils
- cosmic warning signage

### Core tension
The image appears charged, sacred, or revelatory, but is undermined by:
- bodily weirdness
- parody
- cheapness
- instability
- cartoon compression

### Best emotional range
- fake revelation
- goofy cosmic dread
- symbol overload
- cheap transcendence

### Main drift risk
Turning into:
- serious occult art
- sleek esoteric branding
- mystical poster sincerity

### Fix
Add parody, asymmetry, sweet contamination, or symbolic stupidity.

---

## 3. BODY GOBLIN BRANCH

### Main emphasis
- little mutant creatures
- pathetic anatomy
- blob beings
- face-creatures
- weird little limbs
- gross-funny body logic

### Core tension
Creatures feel:
- awkward
- lovable
- wrong
- chemically stunned
- body-made
- not cool

### Best emotional range
- freak tenderness
- gross-funny weirdness
- pathetic creaturehood

- soft body absurdity

### Main drift risk
Turning into:
- monster concept art
- polished mascot creatures
- designer toy slickness

### Fix
Keep them awkward, symbolic, and a little embarrassing.

---

## 4. ABSTRACT HALLUCINATION BRANCH

### Main emphasis
- contour bands
- thermal fields
- plasma blobs
- signal soup
- false topographies
- emergence fields

### Core tension

The abstraction is not neutral.
It should feel like:
- almost-body
- almost-symbol
- latent creature
- pressure field
- mutational substrate

### Best emotional range
- chemical wonder
- LSD-logic adjacency
- body-haunted abstraction
- fake cosmic weather

### Main drift risk
Turning into:
- pretty procedural wallpaper
- generic trippy abstraction
- detached shader beauty

### Fix
Increase emergence pressure and body-glyph potential.

---

## 5. CUTE NECROSIS BRANCH

### Main emphasis
- skulls
- sweet-morbid icons
- hearts and bones
- decorative death motifs
- cute decay

### Core tension
Death iconography is softened, toyed with, and contaminated by sweetness.

### Best emotional range
- cute-morbid
- sweet eerie
- decorative death
- soft wrongness

### Main drift risk
Turning into:
- goth merch
- horror branding
- edgy cute cliché

### Fix
Keep it weirder, softer, and less cool.

---

## 6. GLITCH STICKER BRANCH

### Main emphasis
- signal damage
- digital cheapness
- pixel swim
- threshold shimmer
- screen-feel
- badge/sticker modularity

### Core tension
Digital artifacts support:
- symbolic mutation
- low-fi vitality
- synthetic wrongness

Not just generic glitch aesthetics.

### Best emotional range
- digital hallucination
- cheap synthetic intensity
- rude screen magic

### Main drift risk
Turning into:
- cyber-glitch art
- techwear aesthetics
- abstract digital coolness

### Fix
Reintroduce body glyphs, Sweet Corruption, and fake revelation.

## BRANCH OVERLAP ZONES

The strongest outputs often live between branches.

### Good overlap examples
- Sweet Corruption + Mock Revelation
- Body Goblin + Abstract Hallucination
- Cute Necrosis + Mock Revelation
- Glitch Sticker + Abstract Hallucination
- Sweet Corruption + Body Goblin

## FINAL RULE

Substyle branches should feel like:
different hormonal moods of the same mutant symbolic organism

not like separate franchises.
