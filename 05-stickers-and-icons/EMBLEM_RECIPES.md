# EMBLEM RECIPES

This file contains recipes for making emblem-like, badge-like, sticker-like, or logo-like units in the
Garbage Enlightenment style.

These are useful for:
- poster centers
- sticker sheets
- shirt graphics
- fake cult logos
- icon systems
- little symbolic panels
- recurring motif packs

## Core principle

A Garbage Enlightenment emblem should feel like:

- a symbol
- a sticker
- a warning sign
- a body part pretending to be a logo
- a cheap sacred badge
- a fake revelation object
- a decorative contamination unit

It should not feel like:
- corporate branding
- luxury occult merch
- polished vector logo work
- flat-design cleanliness

## RECIPE 1 — BODY GLYPH EMBLEM

### Formula
Choose one major body glyph:
- eye
- mouth
- hand
- skull
- face
- blob

Then compress it into:
- a simplified graphic unit
- rude black outlines
- strong silhouette
- synthetic flat color
- one contamination element
- one support motif

### Example
A floating mouth emblem with black cavity center, acid pink lip shape, tiny stars around it, and
one fake sacred ring to add symbolic over-meaning

### Formula
Use:
- contour bands
- thermal zones
- plasma shapes

But constrain them into:
- a readable shape
- a badge boundary
- a field that hints at body emergence

### Example
A circular contour-band emblem that nearly resolves into a face and then falls back into abstract
psychedelic topography

## EMBLEM COMPONENT STACK

A good emblem often contains:

### 1. Core shape
Circle, oval, heart, triangle, blob, badge silhouette

### 2. Main symbol
Eye, mouth, hand, skull, face, rainbow, etc.

### 3. Contaminant
Cute motif, body glyph, abstract field, fake sacred sign

### 4. Frame
Halo, ring, contour bands, stars, corner clutter

### 5. Cheapness cue
Rude outline, asymmetry, anti-polish color, slight weirdness

## EMBLEM MOOD TYPES

### Cute emblem
Heart, star, rainbow, cute beast, contaminated lightly

### Gross emblem
Mouth, teeth, blob, tongue, body-heavy symbol

### Fake sacred emblem
Eye, hand, pyramid, aura ring, parody charge

### Creature emblem
Small body goblin or face-creature as badge

### Hallucination emblem
Contour field or abstract unit that almost becomes a glyph

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

An emblem becomes more Garbage Enlightenment-specific when it feels:
- simple in silhouette
but
- semantically unstable on inspection

Example:
- star that wants to be an eye
- mouth that reads like a warning sign
- hand that feels like a portal
- rainbow that feels bodily

That is a strong emblem version of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic.

## FINAL RULE

A Garbage Enlightenment emblem should feel like:

**a cheap sacred sticker that seems more meaningful and less trustworthy the longer you stare
at it**
