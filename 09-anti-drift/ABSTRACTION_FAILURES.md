# ABSTRACTION FAILURES

This file lists common ways abstract work fails inside Garbage Enlightenment.

This matters because abstraction is a major subsystem in the repo, but also one of the easiest
places for drift and deadness to sneak in.

## Core principle

Garbage Enlightenment abstraction should feel like:
- symbolic substrate
- body-haunted field
- emergence pressure
- cheap hallucination system
- almost-readability
- mutational weather

If it loses those, it often becomes generic and dead very fast.

## FAILURE TYPE 1 — PRETTY WALLPAPER

### Symptoms
- smooth gradients
- elegant flow
- decorative beauty
- nice colors
- no stakes

### Why it fails
It looks pleasant but carries no symbolic pressure.

### Fix
Add:
- latent face logic
- glyph emergence
- contour conflict
- threshold harshness
- body implication

---

## FAILURE TYPE 2 — GENERIC PSYCHEDELIC MUSH

### Symptoms
- rainbow fog
- random swirls
- “trippy” without structure
- no contour discipline
- no figure potential

### Why it fails

The abstraction is too vague and too broad.
It lacks the repo’s actual nervous system.

### Fix
Use:
- contour bands
- pressure zones
- face pressure
- body-glyph hints
- threshold emergence

---

## FAILURE TYPE 3 — SHADER BEAUTY DRIFT

### Symptoms
- technically nice field
- good motion maybe
- visually smooth
- no body weirdness
- no symbolic contamination

### Why it fails
The system may be technically cool but not stylistically specific.

### Fix
Contaminate with:
- eyes
- mouths
- hands
- creature pressure
- fake revelation framing
- cheapness

---

## FAILURE TYPE 4 — EMPTY SIGNAL DAMAGE

### Symptoms
- glitch textures
- scanlines
- compression
- digital dirt

But:
- no emergence
- no body logic
- no symbolic role

### Why it fails
Digital damage alone does not equal Garbage Enlightenment.

### Fix
Make the damage support:
- glyph emergence
- field instability
- symbolic over-meaning
- body-haunted threshold events

---

## FAILURE TYPE 5 — TOO MAJESTIC

### Symptoms
- beautiful aura space
- spiritual cosmic grandeur
- reverent visual atmosphere
- elegant transcendence field

### Why it fails
This pushes abstraction toward visionary art instead of rude symbolic hallucination.

### Fix
Cheapen it.
Flatten it.
Add body weirdness.
Break the nobility.

---

## FAILURE TYPE 6 — TOO CLEANLY ABSTRACT

### Symptoms
- abstraction never threatens to become a figure
- no pareidolia pressure
- no body hints
- no almost-symbols

### Why it fails
Figure-pattern bridge logic is missing.

### Fix
Add:
- latent face pressure
- eye-like nodes
- cavity seams

- threshold blob structures
- pattern with creature memory

---

## FAILURE TYPE 7 — TOO DEAD TONALLY

### Symptoms
- good form but no emotional charge
- field feels inert
- no weird tenderness, threat, or fake significance
- abstract but emotionally blank

### Why it fails
Garbage Enlightenment fields should feel emotionally loaded.

### Fix
Decide a tone:
- chemical wonder
- fake revelation
- sweet wrongness
- body dread
- soft menace
- symbolic pressure

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Abstract work gets much stronger when it uses **cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic** in a
structural way:
- one contour implies multiple identities
- patterns carry over-meaning
- face pressure emerges and recedes
- significance is unstable
- abstraction feels like it is trying to become a symbol

That is what keeps it from becoming generic “trippy art.”
