# SIGIL NOTES

This file focuses on sigil-like forms, fake symbolic marks, and doodled sacred-looking insignia
inside Garbage Enlightenment.

These are not meant to be:
- actual ritual orthodoxy
- serious occult design systems
- luxury esoteric branding
- perfect ceremonial seals

They are meant to be:
- suggestive
- weird
- body-adjacent
- fake-sacred

- playful
- low-fi
- symbolically loaded
- slightly unreliable

## Core principle

A Garbage Enlightenment sigil should feel like:
- it might mean something
- it probably means too much
- it definitely isn’t fully trustworthy
- it may also secretly be a body part, portal, or joke

## GOOD SIGIL TRAITS

- asymmetry allowed
- hand-done or rude energy
- body-glyph contamination
- emblematic compactness
- over-significant but low-budget vibe
- usable as decorative support or main icon

## BAD SIGIL TRAITS

- over-clean geometry
- too much ceremonial polish
- hyper-legible authority
- luxury occult branding energy
- sacred symmetry without weirdness
- emotionally sterile design

## SIGIL SOURCES

Good inputs for fake sigils:
- eye shapes
- mouth seams
- hand forms
- stars
- rings
- contour loops
- tiny heads
- body blobs
- little warning marks
- cute motifs gone strange

## SIGIL MODES

### 1. BODY SIGIL

A sigil that clearly contains body logic.

### 2. REVELATION SIGIL
A fake sacred insignia with aura-ring or eye-heavy emphasis.

### 3. SWEET SIGIL
A cute motif pushed into symbolic excess.

### 4. PORTAL SIGIL
A threshold-like symbol with opening/cavity logic.

### 5. SWARM SIGIL
A sigil made of many tiny repeated glyphs.

## HOW TO MAKE A SIGIL FEEL ON-BRAND

- contaminate it with body logic
- keep it slightly rude
- avoid excessive elegance
- let it imply more than one reading
- allow emotional weirdness
- let it function like a sticker, omen, or low-budget holy mark

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Sigils become especially Garbage Enlightenment-specific when:
- they feel over-significant without being fully readable
- they imply several symbolic jobs at once
- they almost become faces or body signs
- they feel like a compressed hallucination-event rather than a design asset

This is a strong static use of cartoonized LSD-hallucination logic.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- fake sacred sigil
- body-contaminated symbol mark
- low-budget occult seal
- over-significant doodle glyph
- hand-drawn revelation insignia
- semi-legible sacred nonsense mark
- body-haunted sigil emblem

## FINAL RULE

A Garbage Enlightenment sigil should feel like:
**a sketchy little sacred lie with a body problem**

# TONE MIXING NOTES

This file explains how tonal mixtures work inside Garbage Enlightenment.

This is important because the style becomes weak very fast when its tone is too pure.

## Core principle

Garbage Enlightenment thrives on tonal friction.

It rarely wants to be:
- only cute
- only gross
- only sacred
- only funny
- only eerie
- only abstract
- only pathetic

It usually wants:
- overlap

- contradiction
- emotional instability
- mixed signals
- symbolic ambiguity with feeling

## MAIN TONE COMPONENTS

### Cute absurdity
Sweet, toy-like, decorative, soft, silly.

### Freak tenderness
Awkward, vulnerable, lovable, wrong, emotionally exposed.

### Gross-funny body weirdness
Mouths, blobs, teeth, tongues, body jokes, soft disgust.

### Fake revelation
Symbolic over-meaning, cheap transcendence, fake sacred charge.

### Goofy cosmic dread
Eerie, watchful, over-significant, but still absurd.

### Chemical wonder
Bright, hallucinatory, emotionally vivid, synthetic ecstasy.

## STRONG TONE MIXES

---

## 1. Cute + Gross

### Why it works
This is the engine of Sweet Corruption.

### Example
A rainbow with a mouth.
A heart with teeth.
A cute creature with body weirdness.

---

## 2. Sacred + Ridiculous

### Why it works
This powers Mock Revelation.

### Example
A hand haloed like a saint but slightly stupid.

An eye pyramid that feels charged but also low-rent.

---

## 3. Eerie + Tender

### Why it works
This makes creatures and icons feel emotionally sticky.

### Example
A pathetic blob face that is unsettling but lovable.

---

## 4. Abstract + Bodily

### Why it works
This powers figure-pattern bridge logic.

### Example
A contour field that feels like a face trying to happen.

---

## 5. Sweet + Fake Significant

### Why it works
This produces over-meaningful cute imagery.

### Example
A star cluster that feels spiritually overcommitted.

## WEAK TONE MIXES

These are not always unusable, but they often need help.

### Pure cute
Becomes safe.

### Pure dark
Becomes grim.

### Pure sacred
Becomes sincere in the wrong way.

### Pure gross
Becomes one-note and juvenile without enough emotional complexity.

### Pure abstraction
Becomes decorative.

## HOW TO MIX TONE ON PURPOSE

### Step 1
Choose a dominant tone:
- cute
- body-gross
- fake sacred
- eerie
- abstract wonder

### Step 2
Choose one contaminant tone:
- tenderness
- stupidity
- body weirdness
- sweetness
- menace
- over-significance

### Step 3
Choose one support tone:

- playfulness
- sadness
- cheapness
- fake authority
- decorative charm

This gives the image tonal layers instead of a single flat note.

## LSD-LOGIC NOTE

Tone mixing becomes especially strong when it echoes **cartoonized LSD-hallucination
structure**:
- too much significance attached to simple things
- emotional cross-signals arriving at once
- sweetness and dread sharing the same symbol
- a cute image feeling weirdly profound for no solid reason

This is one of the best ways to keep the style psychologically alive.

## PROMPT PHRASES

Useful phrases:
- cute but over-significant
- eerie and affectionate
- fake sacred with body humor

- chemically vivid tenderness
- decorative menace
- sweet but contaminated
- emotionally mixed symbolic weirdness

## FINAL RULE

A good Garbage Enlightenment image should often feel like:
**it is having more than one emotional truth at the same time and refusing to pick just one**
